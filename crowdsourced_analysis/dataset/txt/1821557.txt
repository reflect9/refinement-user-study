arts, briefly; atlanta symphony's second amphitheater   the atlanta symphony orchestra will open a $35 million, 12,000-seat amphitheater on 45 acres in alpharetta, ga., about 25 miles north of atlanta, the orchestra announced. the new amphitheater, the encore park for the arts, is to open in summer 2008. it will be owned by the woodruff arts center, where the orchestra performs in atlanta, and is to eventually include a visual arts complex and education center. the orchestra will present summer concerts at its current home, the chastain park amphitheater in atlanta, as well as at the new site.  ben sisario
