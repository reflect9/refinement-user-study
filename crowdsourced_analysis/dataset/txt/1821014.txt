paid notice: deaths   roseman, harvey   roseman--harvey, 71 died on january 22nd, 2007 from complications of melanoma. he was the beloved husband of karen; devoted father of lillian and paul, grandfather of alex, jake, max and sam and special friend of rascal, puff & pumpkin. harvey was a lifelong new yorker born in bklyn & graduate of hofstra university. he passed away at home surrounded by love & his devoted family. he was a proprietor of all-ways sportswear, an international buying service for specialty and dept stores. his life was driven by a passion for the business and his wisdom, diligence and forbearance is a testament for his legacy. he was a world traveler who opened the asian markets in the 60's for macy's & other retail operations. in lieu of food/ flowers, donations should be made to nyu melanoma research fund,106 e. 34th st., 9th flr, ny, ny 10016. memorial service will be held at riverside memorial on wed january 24th at 2:45 pm
