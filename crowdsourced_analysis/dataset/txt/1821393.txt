paid notice: deaths   bernstein, howard   bernstein--howard, 63, died of cancer in nyack, ny, on january 15, 2007. dr. bernstein received his doctorate in history from columbia university in 1972. a prolific writer and gifted teacher, he touched the lives of generations of students over the course of twenty years as a professor at wesleyan university in connecticut. he also taught at brooklyn college, the city university of new york, york university and yale university. he spent the last four years of his life as a mentor and educator at suffield academy. he was a dynamic, engaging, thoughtful and caring teacher who believed there were no wrong answers in his classroom. he delighted in opening minds to new ideas and to critical thinking. he is survived by his immediate family joan bernstein, hilary bernstein, christina bernstein, xiao-bin ji, and deborah dowell, extended family, friends and students, and students who became family and close friends. the memorial service will be held in new york city on february 6th. please contact the family at: howardmemory@gmail.com if you would like more information.
