metro briefing | new jersey: newark: man guilty in fatal crash   an old bridge man was convicted of vehicular homicide and manslaughter in state superior court yesterday for a 2004 crash in newark that killed two sisters, said christopher iu, an essex county assistant prosecutor. the man, kevin mcgrath, 43, hit the sisters, venessa pereira, 19, and maressa farias, 13, both of newark, as they walked into the street. mr. mcgrath, who was driving 50 miles per hour in a 25 m.p.h. zone, told investigators that he had fallen asleep behind the wheel.  john holl
