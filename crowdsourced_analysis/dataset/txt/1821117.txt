metro briefing | new york: queens: 2 dead, and murder-suicide is suspected   a 30-year-old man and his 21-year-old female companion were found fatally shot yesterday in an apartment in sunnyside. the police believe the man shot the woman before turning a gun on himself. the woman's mother, who lived with her daughter in the apartment on 49th street, discovered the two shot in the head about 7 p.m. a .22-caliber gun was found near the man, whose name was not immediately released pending family notification, the police said. the woman, gabriella alcivir, had requested a restraining order against the man after she said he assaulted her in september, a law enforcement official said. but the two had since reconciled, the official said.  jennifer 8. lee
