from banker to baker in tribeca   you can't open a bakery in new york without cupcakes these days, so rachel thebault, a former investment banker who has opened tribeca treats, offers small ones ($2 each) aswirl with chocolate, vanilla, coconut, espresso, peanut butter, caramel or marshmallow frostings. there is even a red velvet one with cream cheese icing. her sandwich cookies are also modest in size and come in chocolate, vanilla, peanut and graham cracker with various cream fillings, $1.25 each or $18 a dozen. chocolates and cakes round out the inventory. the shop also sells gift items. it's at 94 reade street (church street), (212) 571-0500.  food stuff
