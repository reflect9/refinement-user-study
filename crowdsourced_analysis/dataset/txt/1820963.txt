national briefing | south: louisiana: congressman to run for governor   representative bobby jindal, republican of louisiana, said he would enter the 2007 governor's race, his second attempt at winning the office. mr. jindal will be running against a weakened incumbent, gov. kathleen babineaux blanco, a democrat. ms. blanco defeated mr. jindal by four percentage points in 2003, but her standing was damaged after hurricane katrina. mr. jindal, 35, has also been the secretary of the louisiana department of health and hospitals and an assistant secretary at the federal department of health and human services in the bush administration.  adam nossiter
