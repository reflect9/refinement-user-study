paid notice: deaths   raicer, julius b.   raicer--julius b., 87. beloved husband, father, grandfather, friend and colleague, julius died peacefully at his home in morristown, nj on january 9, 2007, surrounded by family. julius was a wwii veteran, president of dover furniture, partner in whippany manor, and active in commercial real estate development. julius is survived by sons robert and alan, daughter leslie, and grandchildren, mei-an, geoffrey, and alex. his wife of 56 years, helen delello raicer, died in 2002. in lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the julius b. raicer memorial fund, c/o leslie raicer, 13 atilda ave., dobbs ferry, ny 10522
