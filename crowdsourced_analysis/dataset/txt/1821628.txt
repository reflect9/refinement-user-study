paid notice: deaths   fowler, trudye hathcote   fowler--trudye hathcote, 97, of alexandria, va died on tuesday, january 22, 2008. she is predeceased by her husband henry hamill fowler and her son henry h. fowler jr. she was mother to marianne smith and susan fowler-gallagher, sister of lunette hayes and luneil dutko. she is also survived by four grandchildren and 13 great-grandchildren. a memorial service will be held in the spring at christ church in alexandria, va. in lieu of flowers, a donation may be made to the juvenile diabetes research foundation, 120 wall street, 19th floor, nyc, ny 10005 or jdrf.org.
