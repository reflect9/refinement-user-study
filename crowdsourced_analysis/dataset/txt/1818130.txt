metro briefing | new york: bronx: hospital closing blocked   a state supreme court judge has issued a temporary restraining order halting the state from proceeding with plans to close westchester square medical center in the east bronx. the ruling last week by justice mary ann brigantti-hughes, will remain in effect until a hearing on jan. 29. westchester square is one of nine hospitals statewide that a state panel recommended be closed. several other hospitals are seeking to reverse the commission's decision.  timothy williams
