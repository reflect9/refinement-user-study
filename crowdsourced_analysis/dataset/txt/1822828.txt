corrections   because of an editing error, an article on thursday about the future of relations between turkey and armenia following the funeral for hrant dink, a turkish-armenian newspaper editor killed in istanbul on jan. 19, misidentified the issue between the two countries that turkey has said should be examined by a governmental history commission. it is the 1915 armenian genocide -- not the political status of nagorno-karabakh, a region that armenia claims but that turkey recognizes as part of azerbaijan.
