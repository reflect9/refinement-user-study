paid notice: deaths   enriquez, rogelio, m.d.   enriquez--rogelio, m.d. passed away peacefully at the beginning of his 81st year on january 14, 2007. as a physician, he ministered to the cuban-american community. he was the pillar of his extensive family whom he revered. roger was a grand master bridge player. his generosity, kindness and caring will be deeply missed by those whose lives he touched. he is survived by his devoted nephew wilfred, niece ileana, brother daniel and edith, and numerous family and friends.
