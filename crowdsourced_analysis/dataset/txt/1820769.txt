paid notice: deaths   gifford, harriette alper   gifford--harriette alper, of new york city and manhasset, ny, died peacefully at home sunday january 21st, 2007 at age 88. beloved mother of richard perse and ilsa perse, loving grandmother of tobias, allison, zoey, cherished mother-in-law of margie perse, caring friend and stepmother of gail rudin, and devoted wife of the late samuel gifford. harriette was a woman of elegance, grace, charm, and wit. she took deep pleasure in living life to its fullest. she was an ardent supporter and connoisseur of the arts, and contributed to many humanitarian causes. she profoundly touched the lives of those she loved and will be forever missed. a memorial to celebrate harriette's life will be held thursday, january 25th. please call 516-365-8568 for details. in lieu of flowers please send donations to hospice care network.
