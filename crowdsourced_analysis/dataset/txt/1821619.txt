paid notice: deaths   bristol, herman g.   bristol--herman g., 75 years young, died suddenly on january 20. he is survived by his wife, gloria, his daughters, elyce and cara, four grandchildren and two stepsons. pioneer african american salesman at ibm, lifelong horseman and lover of music, herman was grateful every day for his life and blessings until the day of his transition. though filled with sorrow, we are grateful for, and inspired by, his life. for funeral information, call roy l. gilmore funeral home after monday 718-528-7765.
