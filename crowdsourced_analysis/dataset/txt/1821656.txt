paid notice: deaths   solomon, anthony m   solomon--anthony m. the staff of the harvard university art museums mourn the loss of esteemed donor and collections committee member, anthony m. solomon. in addition to his long career as economist, statesman, diplomat, and international banker and industrialist, we celebrate his legacy as a collector and benefactor of the arts. the art museums express their deepest sympathies to mr. solomon's family and friends. thomas w. lentz, elizabeth and john moors cabot director. robert d. mowry, alan j. dworsky curator of chinese art. harvard university art museums
