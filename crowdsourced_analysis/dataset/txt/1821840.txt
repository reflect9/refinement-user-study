national briefing | south: tennessee: man killed in building collapse   one construction worker was killed and another injured when a one-story brick building collapsed in nashville, the police said. the workers -- richard whiteshield, 57, of clewiston, fla., who was killed, and john kemper of hendersonville, tenn., who was injured -- were part of a crew that was using a jackhammer to dig up a gas main to the building, which was being demolished, said kristin mumford, a spokeswoman for the metropolitan police department. the building, in the city's berry hill neighborhood, had been closed for some time, said kim lawson, the deputy fire chief.  theo emery
