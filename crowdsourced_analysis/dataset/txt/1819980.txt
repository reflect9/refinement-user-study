paid notice: deaths  tumkovsky, antonina   tumkovsky--antonina. the entire school of american ballet family deeply mourns the death of antonina tumkovsky; we shall all miss her indomitable spirit. tumey was a favorite teacher at the school for more than fifty years. her daily classes were exacting and exhausting, speedy and precise, whether for eight-yearold beginners or for advanced women. never one to overanalyze, she simply demanded that her students ''do, do, do.'' peter martins, chairman of faculty kay mazzo, co-chairman marjorie van dercook, executive director
