paid notice: deaths   weixel, david   weixel--david, 91, on december 31, 2006. beloved husband of his late wife, lola. loving father of miriam and eugene (marbella); grandfather of joanna, rose and goldie (chad); step-grandfather of yorknewsa and zadaai; brother, uncle, greatuncle and friend to many. a soldier during wwii, he was an infantryman on the anzio beachhead and later worked in combat zones as a reconnaissance car crewman, making topographical sketches of terrain and using his knowledge of french, italian and german to interrogate prisoners. in civilian life, he was a film editor, artist and sculptor. we will miss this brave and gentle man. memorial service to be announced.
