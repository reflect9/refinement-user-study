paid notice: deaths   posner, roy e.   posner--roy e. a financial advisor to the new york giants for the past 15 years, suffered a fatal heart attack 90 minutes after saturday's december 30th game. he was 73. survived by his daughter and son-in-law karen and peter montano and four grandchildren samantha, parker, jason, and sara rose, daughter sheryl posner, as well as his former wife donna posner, with whom he remained great friends. memorial services will be held on tuesday january 9th at 11:30am at frank e. campbell, the funeral chapel 1076 madison ave at 81 street. in lieu of flowers donations may be made to the roy e. posner memorial fund c/o cheverus high school, 267 ocean ave. portland maine 04103.
