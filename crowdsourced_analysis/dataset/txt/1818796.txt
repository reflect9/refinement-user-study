marcia greenblatt, morgan hayes   marcia lynne greenblatt, the daughter of cynthia greenblatt and howard greenblatt of wethersfield, conn., was married last evening to morgan joseph hayes, a son of britt m. hayes and dr. joseph g. hayes of new york. rabbi steven j. mason performed the ceremony in the chapel at trinity college in hartford, from which the bride graduated.  mrs. hayes 26, is a manager of corporate events at forbes.com, the online division of forbes media in new york. her father, who is retired, was the president and an owner of better brands, a supplier of food and services to institutions, in windsor, conn. her mother teaches history at wethersfield high school.  mr. hayes, also 26, is a third-year law student at fordham. he graduated from cornell. his mother is a psychotherapist in new york. his father, an internist and cardiologist, is an associate dean at weill medical college of cornell university and the herbert j. and ann l. siegel distinguished professor of clinical medicine.  weddings/celebrations
