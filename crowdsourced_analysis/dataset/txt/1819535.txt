metro briefing | new york: bronx: man arrested in fatal stabbing   a bronx man was arrested yesterday in connection with the fatal stabbing of a woman in her tremont apartment building early sunday, the police said. the man, benjamin waters, 33, another tenant of the woman's building on 180th street, has been charged with murder, the police said. he is accused of stabbing carolyn vargas, 28, during a small party in the building, the police said.
