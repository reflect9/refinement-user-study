paid notice: deaths   van hengel, maarten   van hengel--maarten, died peacefully at home with his loving family in scarborough ny on december 29, 2006. born in amsterdam, the netherlands, on march 29, 1927. survived by his wife of 55 years, the former drusilla drake riley; his four children and their spouses: maarten and claudia van hengel; ginny and john loughlin; hugh and maryann van hengel; and drusilla van hengel and joshua patlak; and his five grandchildren: peter, christina, katherine, emily, and riley. memorial service at scarborough presbyterian church on saturday january 6, 2007 at 11:30 am. private interment. donations may be made in maarten's memory to phelps memorial hospital, sleepy hollow or the netherlands-america foundation, new york city. $(6$)van hengel--maarten. the partners of brown brothers harriman & co. mourn the passing of their long time friend and partner, maarten van hengel. maarten joined the firm in 1950 and spent 56 years making it a better place. his wisdom, wit, and energy will be missed. our sincerest condolences are sent to his wife dru and his whole family. for the partners michael w. mcconnell
