paid notice: deaths   towbin, adele may   towbin--adele may. beloved adele died on january 9, age 90. professionally, adele taught physical education at erasmus hall high school and served as its department chair in the l960s. she graduated from brooklyn college in l937 and recieved the post 50th lifetime achievement and milton fisher l938 second harvest awards in l997. fiercely independent, adele was an activist in greenwich village's caring comm, the audabon society, a world federalist, and the preservation of yiddish culture. adele also loved concerts at music mountain in ct. surviving adele are niece beryl towbin esecover and nephews david towbin and robert lyons, and many grand nieces and nephews. a memorial will be on january 28th at the plaza jewish comm chapel on 91st/amsterdam, at 2pm.
