metro briefing | new york: manhattan: new post for public schools   schools chancellor joel i. klein yesterday announced the creation of a new administrative position: chief executive for parent engagement, charged with ensuring a ''greater level of collaboration with parents and community groups that serve them.'' the chief executive, who is expected to be named this spring, will report directly to the chancellor. the announcement came at mr. klein's testimony at a city council hearing, at which he was questioned on plans to change the school system.  elissa gootman
