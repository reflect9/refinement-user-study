paid notice: deaths   elias, albert j.   elias--albert j., passed away peacefully at his home on jupiter island, fl at the age of 86 on saturday , january 13th, 2007. he leaves behind his loving wife of 62 years rea, sons albert l. and william elias, grandchildren vanessa and lee krelstein, jon and ben elias. born in nyc on september 21st, 1920, son of the late albert i. and helen cassatt elias, educated at mcburney prep., and university of virginia class of 1942. a veteran of wwii, he served his country in the us army from june 1942 to january 1945, achieving the rank of captain, amphibious engineers, with tours in africa, england, and d-day in normandy, okinawa, and korea. in 1973 he retired as president of the pharmaco group of schering plough. he authored several novels, ''the bowman test'', ''the sonora mutation'', and ''she was eighteen and 1/2''. prior to moving to florida mr. elias resided in greenwich, ct. his final resting place is in columbarium at the arlington cemetery. a private celebration of his life is being held at home on january 27th, 2007, 4-6pm. in lieu of flowers contributions may be made to united cerebral palsy of ny.
