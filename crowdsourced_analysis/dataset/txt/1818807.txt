randi reich, joseph cosentino   randi s. reich, the daughter of lois e. reich and robert s. reich of west palm beach, fla., was married last evening to joseph vito cosentino, a son of mary ellen cosentino and joseph cosentino of washington. rabbi nadia gold officiated at the fairmont washington.  mrs. cosentino, 33, is the provost at strayer university in washington, where she is also the senior vice president for academic administration. she graduated magna cum laude from the university of pennsylvania and received an m.b.a. from harvard.  her father is a partner in loeb & loeb, a law firm in new york. her mother owns music 'n motion, a company that entertains at children's events in west palm beach and rye, n.y.  mr. cosentino, 32, is a development associate at abbey road property group, a new real estate development company in washington. he was until last february a captain in the marine corps reserve and served in iraq. he graduated summa cum laude from american university and received an m.b.a. from the university of southern california.  his mother is a human resources manager at the american chemical society in washington. his father, who retired as a director of human resources at verizon communications in arlington, va., is a consultant in the herndon, va., office of booz allen hamilton.  weddings/celebrations
