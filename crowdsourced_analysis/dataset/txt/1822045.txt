carolyn elsinger, jonathan kuhe   carolyn ann elsinger and jonathan james kuhe were married yesterday at the fifth avenue presbyterian church in new york. the rev. dr. robert j. duncan sr., a united methodist minister, performed the presbyterian ceremony.  mrs. kuhe, 32, is a district sales manager for forest pharmaceuticals in new york. she graduated from denison university. she is a daughter of judith l. elsinger and carl r. elsinger of wyckoff, n.j.  mr. kuhe, 36, works in new york as a vice president for sales of mortgaged-backed securities for hsbc. he graduated from hamilton college. he is a son of rachel kuhe of west hartford, conn., and the late h. james kuhe.  weddings/celebrations
