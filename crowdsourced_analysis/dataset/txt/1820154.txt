paid notice: deaths   king, maxine (macky) b.   king--maxine (macky) b., age 91, died peacefully at home in topsham, me, on january 17, surrounded by family and friends. for 58 years she was director of camp fernwood for girls, begun in 1921 by her parents, dr. and mrs. julius bluhm. active in the american camping association; served as an early president of the maine youth camping association in the 80's; received the halsey gulick award in 1983. beloved by four generations of campers and camp staff; loving mother of michael (claire) and jonathan (kathleen). devoted grandmother of adam, brian (jennifer), gabrielle (michael), and benjamin. great-grandmother to charlie, adelaide and zadie. donations may be made in her name to heifer international or the fernwood foundation.
