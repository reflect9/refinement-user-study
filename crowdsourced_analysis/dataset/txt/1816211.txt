paid notice: deaths   sands, harry, ph.d.   sands--harry, ph.d., aged 89 years, died on january 3, beloved husband, father, grandfather, brother, uncle and colleague. he dedicated his life to people through the practice of psychology. he was the former ceo of the postgraduate center for mental health in nyc to which he devoted his passion and commitment for more than 40 years. he was a boundless inspiration to his family and to the people whose lives he touched. he is survived by his loving wife of 61 years, helene; son, jeff, and wife, lynn, of celebration, fl; son, rick, and wife, colleen, of abington, pa; grandchildren: michael, laura, kevin, jessica, and robbie; brothers, nat and al. services at riverside chapels (212-362-6600), 76th st. and amsterdam ave. on friday january 5 at 11:30am; interment to immediately follow; shiva at the family home in nyc. contributions may be made to the postgraduate center for mental health, nyc, or the american psychological foundation, washington, d.c.
