paid notice: deaths   wallach, ira d.   wallach--ira d. the staff and patients of the center for special studies of new york presbyterian hospital mourn the death of ira wallach. ira was a concerned activist who used philanthropy to support initiatives that had the potential to make a difference. miriam and ira were among our first benefactors, and helped us establish a model of care that has subsequently helped thousands of indigent new yorkers infected with hiv. we will miss our friend. dr. jonathan l. jacobs, executive director dr. samuel t. merrick, medical director mr. gerard ilaria, director
