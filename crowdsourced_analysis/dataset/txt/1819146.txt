london 'rings' cast is announced   the shakespearean actor malcolm storry will play the role of the wizard gandalf in the london production of the $25 million musical "the lord of the rings," the bbc reported.  laura michelle kelly, an olivier award winner for her performance in the title role of "mary poppins" in london, has been cast as the powerful elf queen galadriel.  the show opened last year to unenthusiastic reviews in toronto and is expected to be revised before it makes its london debut on june 19 at the theater royal, drury lane.
