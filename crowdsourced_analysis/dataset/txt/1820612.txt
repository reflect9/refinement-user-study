paid notice: deaths   warner, winifred ingraham   warner--winifred ingraham, died on january 19, 2007. born october 20, 1913, to henry andrews and mary shotwell ingraham of brooklyn and northport, ny. wife of harold lawson warner for 70 years. also survived by daughter mary ann putnam of hilton head, sc, son thomas s. warner of garden city, ny, grandsons jeffrey putnam of miniapolis, mn, jonathan putnam of north potomac, md, christopher putnam of excelsior, mn, and six great-grandchildren: ethan, samuel, eli, sydney, lindsey and matthew. winifred and harold first met in kindergarten at brooklyn friends school in 1918. she was a member of the class of 1931 at packer and graduated from smith college in 1934. an avid gardener, she adored the plantings at the brooklyn botanical garden. a family memorial service will be held later. in lieu of flowers, contributions to your favorite charity would be greatly appreciated.
