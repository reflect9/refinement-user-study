metro briefing | new york: manhattan: remains at ground zero   searchers recovered nine bone fragments that they believe to be human from the world trade center haul road along west street yesterday. four were found at the site and five others as material was being inspected at a sifting center in brooklyn, said ellen borakove, a spokeswoman for the chief medical examiner. the bones ranged in size from one to four inches. since september 2005, 990 human remains have been found around ground zero.  david w. dunlap
