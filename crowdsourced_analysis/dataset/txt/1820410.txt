elisabeth paton, matthew casey   elisabeth anne paton and matthew douglas casey were married last evening in new york. the rev. michael l. lindvall, a presbyterian minister, performed the ceremony at brick presbyterian church, with the rev. george hill, a roman catholic priest, taking part.  mrs. casey, 25, is an interior designer in new york. she graduated from hamilton college and received an associate's degree in interior design from the new york school of interior design. she is the daughter of nancy nation paton and leland bartlett paton of rye, n.y. her father retired as the chief administrative officer of prudential securities in new york.  mr. casey, 32, is an associate in the securities unit of bank of america in new york, working on corporate bonds. he graduated from the university of miami. he is the son of beth casey and j. douglas casey iii of rye. his father is a principal and a managing director at gates capital corporation, an investment banking firm in new york.  weddings/celebrations
