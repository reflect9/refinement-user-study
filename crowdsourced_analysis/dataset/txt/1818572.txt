paid notice: deaths   weinberg, hans   weinberg--hans. the yeshiva university family joins in mourning the passing of a dedicated administrator in the jewish community where he was widely admired. heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife, joyce, to their son, stuart, and to his cherished siblings, yeshiva college and riets alumnus, rabbi norbert (and susan) weinberg, and our devoted stern college for women alumna, marga marx, who with her husband, moses marx, a riets trustee, is a major leader.may the entire family be comforted among the mourners of zion and jerusalem. yeshiva university richard m. joel, president dr. norman lamm, chancellor morry j. weiss, chairman board of trustees julius berman, chair riets board
