paid notice: deaths   bergier, arnold henry   bergier--arnold henry, 92, on friday, january 19th. a sculptor, collagist, and caricaturist, mr. bergier is listed in ''who's who in american art''. his sculptures include portraits from life of prominent persons, works for synagogues and churches, and public buildings. as a young man he sang in the metropolitan opera chorus. he was active in the 1950s' ''save the village'' historic preservation campaign and was recently honored as one of its leaders. a panelist on the long john nebel radio show for many years. an active member of the american movement for world government, veterans for peace, and a lifetime member of veterans of foreign wars. survived by his wife jean kwok, children thomas bergier, wendy ashley, susan bergier, and barbara bergier, five grandchildren, and eight great-grandchildren. a gathering of family and friends will be held saturday, january 27th at the greenwich village funeral home, 199 bleecker st. at 11am. memorial donations may be made in his name to the american movement for world government, 104 paradise harbour blvd, #
