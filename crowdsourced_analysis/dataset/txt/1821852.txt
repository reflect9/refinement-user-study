paid notice: deaths   cross, george r.   cross--george r., died peacefully on january 17, 2007, in punta gorda, fl, at the age of 83. he was born in new york city may 9, 1923. he was a first lieutenant in the us airforce during wwii. he was an attorney and held various senior executive positions in the insurance industry. he was a supreme court attorney. survivors include a large, loving family and many friends. national cremation society is in charge, interment will be in hancock, ny, at a later date.
