best friend? maybe not   to the editor:  re ''taking steps so aging does not mean falling'' (dec. 26): pets, both those owned by seniors and by other people, should be added to the list of potential hazards cited in jane brody's column about seniors falling.  cats and small dogs have a way of getting underfoot, often when least expected, while large dogs that jump up to greet older people can knock them over.  steven d. price  new york
