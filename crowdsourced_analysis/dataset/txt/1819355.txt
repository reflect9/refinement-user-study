paid notice: deaths   griffiths, donna marie   griffiths--donna marie. donna marie griffiths passed away at stamford hospital on january 15, 2007, at the age of 65. daughter of the late john and sadie marchyn, beloved wife of anthony, sister to judy marchyn, loving mother of monique girard and brigitte king, grandmother of celine, chloe and brandon. donna was a graduate of the university of buffalo with a degree in graphic arts, having shown some of her paintings at the grand palais in paris. she was born in ontario, canada and also lived in brazil, italy, spain and france, moving to greenwich, ct in 1983. cremation has taken place. a private service will be held for immediate family members. no flowers please. instead, we ask that you keep her in your prayers.
