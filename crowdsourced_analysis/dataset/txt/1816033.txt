paid notice: deaths   sommerfeld, megan ceara   sommerfeld--megan ceara, loving wife to james lidington, passed away on december 30, 2006, following an unexpected cardiac event at her home in new york city. megan was born on june 27, 1975, daughter of arne and denise sommerfeld, and sister to matthew. megan grew up in nova scotia where she excelled at horseback riding with the halifax junior bengal lancers. in 1997, she graduated from saint mary's university and moved to toronto, to begin a successful career with reuters america, llc. in march 2006, after a move to new york city and a one year posting in india, megan left her position as global head of timeseries data to join standard & poor's as vice president -global data quality. megan lived life to the fullest: spending time with friends, loving manhattan, travelling the world and recording each adventure through her passion for photography. a memorial service will be held in manhattan at 11:00am on friday, january 5th, 2007 at the church of our lady of the rosary, 7 state street, new york, ny. donations in megan's memory can be made to halifax junior bengal lancers (www.bengallancer s.com) or the susan g. komen breast cancer foundation (www.komen.org)
