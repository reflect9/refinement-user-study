national briefing | new england: new hampshire: bacterial meningitis kills student   a 21-year-old university of new hampshire student died of bacterial meningitis on wednesday, and health officials said more than 90 people in seven states had been warned that they might be at risk for the disease. officials said the student, danielle thompson of bangor, me., had gone home during christmas break, where she came into contact with numerous people. they said ms. thompson's death was not related to current meningitis cases in rhode island.  katie zezima
