ariella hochsztein, elliot friedman   ariella aliza hochsztein, a daughter of giselle dershowitz hochsztein and dr. paul hochsztein of woodmere, n.y., is to be married today to elliot judah friedman, a son of barbara macy friedman and dr. harvey y. friedman of englewood, n.j. rabbi shmuel goldin is to perform the ceremony at the woodbury jewish center in woodbury, n.y.  the bride, 24, is a third-year medical student at weill medical college of cornell university in manhattan. she graduated summa cum laude from the university of pennsylvania. her father, a urologist, is a partner in urological consultants of new york and long island, which has offices in manhattan; sunnyside, queens; and lawrence, n.y.  the bridegroom, also 24, is an investment analyst in the manhattan office of sumitomo trust and banking company, the tokyo bank. he graduated from the university of rochester. his mother is a psychotherapist, and his father is an obstetrician and gynecologist at comprehensive women's care, a group practice, both in englewood.  weddings/celebrations
