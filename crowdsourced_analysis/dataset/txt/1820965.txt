metro briefing | new york: manhattan: ex-guard sentenced for sex with inmate   a former federal prison guard was sentenced in federal district court yesterday for having sexual contact with an inmate and making false statements to investigators, prosecutors said. the former guard, nicholas defonte, 49, of staten island, was sentenced to 18 months in prison, said michael j. garcia, the united states attorney in manhattan. mr. garcia said mr. defonte had sexual contact with the female inmate while he supervised the kitchen at the metropolitan correctional center in manhattan in 2001. a jury in april acquitted mr. defonte of two counts of sexual abuse, but convicted him of sexual activity with an inmate.
