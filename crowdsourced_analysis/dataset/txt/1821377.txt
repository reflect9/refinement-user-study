metro briefing | new york: albany: new head of long island power authority   gov. eliot spitzer appointed the suffolk county executive's chief deputy, kevin s. law, left, the new chairman of the long island power authority yesterday. mr. law succeeds richard m. kessel, who headed the agency under govs. mario m. cuomo and george e. pataki. mr. kessel is expected to take on the vacant post of chief executive officer for several months and then retire. mr. law is then expected to succeed him as the chief executive officer, at which point a new chairman will be appointed.  bruce lambert
