'spamalot' in quest of oliviers  the london production of the broadway hit musical ''monty python's spamalot'' led the field with seven nominations, including best new musical, when the competitors for the laurence oliviers, britain's foremost theater awards, were announced yesterday. 'coram boy' broadway bound  the acclaimed english national theater production of ''coram boy'' is to open on broadway at the imperial theater on may 2, after previews beginning on april 16. the play, adapted by helen edmundson from a novel by jamila gavin, tells of two orphans at a home for deserted children: one saved from an african slave ship, the other the abandoned son of the heir to a great estate.
