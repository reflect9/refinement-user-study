paid notice: deaths   fried, morris (murray)   fried--morris (murray). the board of trustees of long beach medical center records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed colleague morris fried, who faithfully served the medical center for 39 years as a member of the board. he had a deep devotion to the medical center and was passionate about his work in furthering the medical center's mission. we extend our deepest sympathies to his beloved wife florence, his sons greg and charles and to his entire family. alfred i. osterland chairman, board of trustees bernard kennedy president, board of trustees douglas l. melzer chief executive officer
