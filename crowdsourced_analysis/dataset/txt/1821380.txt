metro briefing | new york: queens: real estate broker charged   a queens real estate broker, shelly w. cao, was charged yesterday with defrauding investors of more than $1.5 million, the queens district attorney, richard a. brown, said. he said that ms. cao, 40, of bayside, was charged with accepting the money from four businessmen after telling them she would invest in property in upstate new york, but that she used the money to pay off loans. ms. cao's lawyer, thomas mccullough, said that the matter stemmed from a business dispute now in civil court and that his client would plead not guilty.
