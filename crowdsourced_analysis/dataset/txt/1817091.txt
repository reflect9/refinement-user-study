lauren cutson, andrew janian   lauren beth cutson, a daughter of linda rothfarb cutson and gary a. cutson of new york, was married yesterday to andrew michael janian, a son of roberta s. janian and keith r. janian of danvers, mass. rabbi randy c. fleisher performed the ceremony in the graham chapel at washington university in st. louis, from which the bride and bridegroom graduated and where they met.  mrs. janian, 24, is a third-year law student at the university. her father is a partner in pricewaterhousecoopers, the new york accounting and consulting firm.  mr. janian, also 24, is a software designer at scottrade, the online securities brokerage firm in st. louis. his mother is an agent in the boston office of garber travel. his father is an information technology consultant.  weddings/celebrations
