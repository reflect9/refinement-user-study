metro briefing | new york: queens: conviction in police officer's shooting   lasana philbert, 23, has been convicted in state supreme court of assault on a police officer for wounding officer christopher wiesneski in 2005 by grabbing his gun during a struggle, causing it to fire, the queens district attorney, richard a. brown, said yesterday. prosecutors said that officer wiesneski had to retire as a result of his wound. mr. philbert, of cambria heights, faces up to 15 years in prison when he is sentenced next month, mr. brown said.
