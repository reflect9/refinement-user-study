paid notice: deaths   peskin, marion helen horn   peskin--marion helen horn, of riverdale, ny, and formerly of morristown, nj, died peacefully at age 93. she was the beloved wife of the late edward peskin, and is survived by their son charles and his wife lucille, by their grandson eric and his wife samantha, and by their great-grandson jay. marion peskin was a graduate of smith college and yale university, and worked as a research librarian, most recently for schering-plough. she will be missed by her family and friends around the world.
