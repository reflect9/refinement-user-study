metro briefing | new jersey: newark: transit officials propose fare increase   new jersey transit officials yesterday proposed a 10 percent fare increase for bus and train passengers. the officials said the increase would help close a $60 million deficit in the agency's proposed $1.5 billion budget. the agency's board must first vote on the proposed increase, which would take effect in june if approved. hearings will be held in february and march to give the public a chance to comment. the agency last raised bus and rail fares in july 2005.  maria newman
