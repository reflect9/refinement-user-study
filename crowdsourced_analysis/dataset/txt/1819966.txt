metro briefing | new york: white plains: man charged with rape   a manhattan man has been named in a 23-count indictment accusing him of kidnapping and raping a 12-year-old girl in westchester county on dec. 28, prosecutors said yesterday. the man, william davis, 46, of 126th street, is charged with rape, kidnapping and predatory sexual assault, said janet difiore, the westchester district attorney. mr. davis is scheduled to be arraigned today. ms. difiore said mr. davis is accused of luring a brooklyn girl into his car by identifying himself as a police officer, taking her to westchester and raping her.
