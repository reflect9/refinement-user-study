just a simple taxi ride   to the editor:  re ''high-tech gadgets in taxis will cut profits, cabbies say'' (news article, jan. 5):  touch-screen monitors that allow taxi passengers to watch television, get game scores and track their ride on a global positioning system?  not for me, thank you. i would be satisfied if new york taxis added one simple, inexpensive amenity: reading lights.  peter lavigne  new york, jan. 5, 2007
