metro briefing | new york: brooklyn: four shot outside rink   two women and two men were shot early yesterday in front of a roller skating rink just east of prospect park, the police said. gunfire broke out shortly before 1 a.m. outside the empire roller skating center at 200 empire boulevard, the police said. two people were hit in the leg, one in the foot and the fourth in the abdomen, the police said. all four victims, whose names were not released, were in stable condition at kings county hospital center yesterday, the police said. no arrests have been made.  cara buckley
