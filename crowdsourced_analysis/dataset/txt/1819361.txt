paid notice: deaths   meislin, nathan   meislin--nathan, 84, of allentown, pa., died monday after a stroke. a world war ii veteran and lehigh university graduate, he retired in 1988 as president of fountain hill mills, a knitwear manufacturing concern he owned with late father paul and late brother ira. survived by his wife of 58 years, eleanor, daughter carol springer (ralph) and son richard, and grandchildren ian and anna springer. a horseman until 79, he pursued joy with gusto and shared it with all he knew through humor, song and kindness. service 1pm thursday, bachman kulik funeral home, 17th and hamilton sts., allentown. in lieu of flowers, please contribute to american stroke association.
