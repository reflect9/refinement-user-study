arts, briefly; 'cold case' carries the night for cbs   with abc showing repeats, cbs easily drew the most viewers in every hour on sunday night, according to nielsen's estimates. ''cold case,'' the night's most-watched program, garnered 15.1 million viewers at 8 p.m. it was preceded by ''60 minutes'' at 7 (14.3 million), and followed by the hallmark hall of fame movie ''the valley of light,'' with chris klein, which attracted 14.7 million viewers from 9 to 11. abc won the night among adults 18 to 49 and finished second in total viewers. nbc ranked third, as the first live edition of ''grease: you're the one that i want'' posted the series's lowest rating yet, averaging 7.3 million viewers from 7 to 9 p.m. following it ''the apprentice'' improved over its week-ago performance, drawing 8.4 million viewers and finishing no. 2 in the 9 p.m. hour among adults 18 to 49.  benjamin toff
