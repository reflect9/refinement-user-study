paid notice: deaths   kretchmer, alma massetti   kretchmer--alma massetti, 58, born in brescia, italy, died january 10, 2007 in palenville, new york, after a lengthy battle with breast cancer. she lived with a passion for life and beauty. she was a very special, courageous, and dynamic person, a woman of vision and valor. she worked together with her late husband, steven kretchmer, at steven kretchmer design, and helped develop this internationally renowned jewelry design firm into a leader in the industry. she is survived by her beloved daughter, claudia. a fund through the dana-farber cancer institute is being established in alma's memory. please check www.stevenkretchmer.com in the coming weeks for details.
