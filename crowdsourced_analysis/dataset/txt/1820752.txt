metro briefing | new jersey: newark: man dies from burns   a man who the police say was set on fire after an argument on jan. 12 died on saturday, the authorities said. the man, richard burns, 54, was hospitalized at st. barnabas hospital in livingston after suffering burns to more than 22 percent of his body, said paul m. loriquet, a spokesman for the essex county prosecutor. mr. burns' nephew, lamar a. tidwell, 24, was charged with setting the fire, and mr. loriquet said a charge of murder would probably be filed against him today.  john holl
