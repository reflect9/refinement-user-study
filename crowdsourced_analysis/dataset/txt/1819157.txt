paid notice: deaths   mctague, fr. charles hubert   mctague--fr. charles hubert died january 13 in kearny, nj. until recently, he had lived at holy cross church in harrison, nj. born in buffalo, ny january 9, 1920, to charles francis mctague and charlotte le blond, he joined seafarers union of the pacific in 1938. a graduate of seton hall college and darlinton seminary, ''father charlie'' was ordained in 1947 in newark, nj. after serving at several parishes in new jersey, in 1977 he was assigned to the stella maris chapel as port chaplain of port newark, where he visited thousands of ships, started a sea scouts troop and a serenity hour. father charlie was predeceased by his brother david and his sisters ann and joan. he is survived by his niece judith di leo and her daughters abigail and natalie, his nephews paul and daniel, and their children claire, celeste and deniz. visitation will be at holy cross church in harrison beginning at 3pm on wednesday, january 17, with a vigil mass at 7:30pm. the funeral mass will be at 10:30am on thursday, january 18, also at holy cross church. in lieu of flowers, please send donations made out to ''holy cross church,'' 16 church square, harrison nj, 07029, for the father charlie scholarship fund.
