world briefing | americas: ecuador: president's supporters try to storm congress   hundreds of protesters tried to storm congress, forcing violent clashes with the police. the protesters, largely supporters of president rafael correa, a leftist economist, had gathered to criticize an effort by lawmakers to raise their own salaries and to show support for a plan by mr. correa to convene an assembly to rewrite parts of the constitution. tensions have been building between mr. correa and congress.  simon romero
