world briefing | americas: venezuela: 16 dead in prison melee   sixteen inmates were killed as rival gangs clashed at a prison in uribana, in western lara state. violent deaths have plagued the country's prisons, a system with about 19,000 inmates, as the authorities struggle with crowding and a shortage of trained guards. more than 300 inmates were killed in the first nine months of 2006. almost 50 prisoners were killed in 2006 in uribana, a six-year-old prison considered the most violent in venezuela.  simon romero
