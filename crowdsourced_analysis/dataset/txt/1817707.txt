paid notice: deaths   kimball, katherine elizabeth   kimball--katherine elizabeth, of new york, died on january 3, 2007. she was 61 years old. ms. kimball was born in denison, ia, and grew up in manhasset, ny. she attended the school of the holy child, in suffern, ny, and connecticut college for women. over a long career in advertising, she was employed by the wool bureau, wm. esty, and wunderman. until her recent retirement, she served as senior partner and director of creative resources at ogilvy & mather. at liz's request, there will be no public service. her family will gather at a later time for a private remembrance. donations may be made to the central park conservancy, elizabeth kimball memorial fund. ms. kimball is survived by six sisters and brothers: katherine m. kimball, of crawford, co; caroline sandorff, of mesa, az; richard kimball, of new york, ny; alexander kimball, of garden city, ny; molly kimball fendrick, of orlando, fl; and daniel powers kimball, of aberdeen, nc. the beloved aunt of 13 nieces and nephews, she also leaves behind many dear friends. she will be widely and deeply missed.
