greene and devers join millrose field   maurice greene and gail devers, two americans who have won olympic and world championships, were added yesterday to the field for the 100th annual millrose games feb. 2 in madison square garden. greene, 32, will run in the men's 60-meter dash and devers, 40, in the women's 60-meter hurdles.  frank litsky  sports briefing: track and field
