paid notice: deaths   grunschlag, toni   grunschlag--toni, on january 12th after a heroic ten year struggle with cancer, devoted daughter of the late morris and the late celia. beloved sister of rosi, and the late david. loving cousin of rose levy. toni was a renowned concert pianist and teacher with her sister rosi, she performed as a dual pianist internationally. their most recent concerts were in vienna and in steinway hall in new york city in may 2006. toni will be greatly missed by all. funeral monday, january 15th at 12 noon at plaza jewish community chapel, 630 amsterdam avenue at 91st street. shiva will be held at the grunschlag residence.
