a red warmer for winter   old-timers love to pass a january evening with a good book, an armchair, fresh walnuts and a warming glass or two of fonseca bin no. 27 nonvintage port.  sipped slowly, this smooth, sweet red yields lingering flavors of prunes, raisins and spicy fruitcake. it hints at the spectrum of subtler flavors awaiting buyers of vintage-dated ports, which can be expensive.  a popular portuguese staple, it costs $16.99 at 67 wine, 179 columbus avenue (68th street).  howard g. goldberg  wine under $20
