the sound of silence   to the editor:  is music in restaurants a sore issue? it certainly is. (''music and food? the pros step in,'' jan. 3.) here's a novel idea: no music at all. what makes dining in paris such a joy is not only the food but the blessed absence of music that diners don't get to choose and the volume of which they can't control.  ann j. kirschner  brooklyn, n.y.
