paid notice: deaths   morris, betty osgood   morris--betty osgood, comic dancer, teacher and extraordinary performer died on january 22nd at the age of 91. gifted with many talents, she graduated from radcliffe college-phi beta kappa. she studied dance in boston, came to n.y. danced in the charles wiedman company where she created distinguished comic roles, worked under john butler on early television dance programs, worked in hollywood dancing in the movie ''carousel''. she was invited to join the paper bag players where both children and adults became avid fans. predeceased by her husband carl. she is survived by a son, dan, a daughter, mai, and a granddaughter, katelyn. she will be greatly missed by her loving family, everyone who worked with her, and everyone who ever saw her perform. judith martin the paper bag players
