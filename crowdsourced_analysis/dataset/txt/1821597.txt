metro briefing | new york: manhattan: $253 million for 9/11 memorial   mayor michael r. bloomberg, who is also chairman of the world trade center memorial foundation, told the foundation's board yesterday that the foundation had raised $253.8 million toward its $300 million goal. almost $52 million, about one-fifth of the total raised so far, has been pledged in the last six weeks. a spokeswoman for the foundation declined to identify any of the new contributors. the memorial is expected to cost more than $500 million.  david w. dunlap
