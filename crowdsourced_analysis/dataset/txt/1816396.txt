metro briefing | new york: yonkers: doctor found fatally shot   a 46-year-old physician was found fatally shot on his bedroom floor yesterday afternoon, the yonkers police said. the body of the physician, dr. leandro lozada, who had a practice in the bronx, was discovered by a friend around 3 p.m. at 43 brendon hill road, the police said. they said that dr. lozada lived alone and that there was no sign of forced entry or a weapon.  jennifer 8. lee
