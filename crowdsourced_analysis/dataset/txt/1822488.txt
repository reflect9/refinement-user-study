paid notice: deaths   moyano, maricla   moyano--maricla, 67, writer, died january 1, 2007, san francisco. born february 3, 1939, cordoba, argentina. feminist visionary, icarus, daughter of bilitis 1969, there at the founding of the nyc gay movement, always ahead of her time. loved and remembered by susan, laura, julian; sister magda, brother lewis and the many members of her extended family. funeral on february 3, 2007 at grace episcopal church, bremo bluff, va.
