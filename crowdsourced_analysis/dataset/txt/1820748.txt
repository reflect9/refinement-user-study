metro briefing | new york: north bellmore: two killed in accident   two people were killed and a third was critically injured when their honda accord careered off the southern state parkway near newbridge road in nassau county just before 2:30 a.m. yesterday, according to the new york state police. the driver, juan benitez, 22, of brentwood, and a 16-year-old female front-seat passenger, whose identity was not disclosed, were pronounced dead at the scene, the police said. a 17-year-old male passenger, whose identity was also not disclosed, was ejected from the back seat and sustained a head injury. he was in critical condition late yesterday at nassau university medical center in east meadow, the police said. the cause of the accident remained under investigation yesterday, the police said.  thomas j. lueck
