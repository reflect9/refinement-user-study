metro briefing | new york: manhattan: prostitution sentence   the operator of an escort service in tribeca was sentenced to a year and a half to three years in prison yesterday for money laundering and attempted promotion of prostitution. judge daniel p. fitzgerald sentenced the operator, jason itzler, 39, under the terms of a plea agreement. ''i've grown up, i've matured, i've learned a lot,'' mr. itzler, who once proclaimed himself to be ''king of all pimps'' in an interview with new york magazine, told the court. ''i will never run an escort agency again.''  colin moynihan
