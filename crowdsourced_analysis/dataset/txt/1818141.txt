paid notice: deaths   doctor, daniel w. md   doctor--daniel w. md, died on january 10th in palm beach fl at age 79. dr. daniel doctor devoted his life to his family and his profession. he was born and raised in the bronx, ny. he attended the bronx high school of science and earned both a bachelors and masters of science at new york university. he was a graduate of new york medical college and completed his residency in ophthalmology at manhattan eye, ear and throat hospital where he became an attending. dr. daniel doctor and his wife, dr. judith doctor moved to greens farms, ct in 1961, where they raised their four children, laura, david, leslie and jonathan. he was honored by his colleagues and appointed president of the fairfield county medical society, followed by president of the connecticut state medical society. he began his practice of ophthalmology 50 years ago and was joined by his wife, dr. judith doctor. together, they set a standard of excellence of care in their community. as a physician, his outstanding principles and ethical standards were the foundation for the way he served his patients. his wife, four children and 13 grandchildren survive him. in lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the norwalk hospital foundation, 34 maple street, norwalk, ct 06856. services will be held on sunday, january 14th in palm beach florida at menorah gardens and funeral chapel.
