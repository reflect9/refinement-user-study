metro briefing | new york: shirley: woman dies in house fire   a woman died yesterday morning in a house fire, the suffolk county police said. the woman, delores hutt, 57, was trapped in an upstairs bathroom during the fire at a house on saint george drive west, in shirley, long island, the police said. according to the police, ms. hutt's sister, dorothy landau, was making tea about 4:30 a.m. when she saw flames coming from a blanket on the couch. an investigation continued yesterday, but the police said the fire did not appear to be suspicious.
