metro briefing | new york: brooklyn: arrest in brooklyn crash   jiankai huang of alexandria, va., was arrested yesterday after crashing his car into a pickup truck and a van and hitting three pedestrians in brooklyn, the police said. mr. huang, 29, was charged with driving while intoxicated, reckless endangerment and driving with a suspended license, the police said. they said that mr. huang was driving east on 65th street at 8:15 a.m. when he hit the truck, which was heading north on fort hamilton parkway, and then the van, which was stopped at a red light. the three injured pedestrians, a woman of 26 and two boys, 13 and 11, were hospitalized, the police said. mr. huang's passenger, a 20-year-old woman, was hospitalized with head injuries, and the other drivers for evaluation, the police said.  cara buckley
