paid notice: deaths   lippin, elliot   lippin--elliot, age 79, died january 29. survived by wife rochelle and former wife lucille. father to naomi (ramona), paula (mitchell), samuel (barbara), julie (patty), david, james (susan), grandfather to miriam, daniel, kevin, eric, ella. president, long time visionary, and benefactor of camp sussex. president, american fur institute and fur salesmans association. graduate of c.w. post (magna cum laude), historian, teacher, mentor, stimpson junior high, long island. lover and patron of the arts. activist, athlete, musician and voracious reader. deep appreciation of all of nature's grandeur, land and sea. new york times crossword puzzle in pen. new york city marathon runner. president of the knights of essex a.k.a. aye ''u'' boys, brooklyn ny. you lived life to its fullest, you will live forever in our hearts and memories. donations made to camp sussex care of richard schwartz e.b.g. 250 park ave, new york, ny 10177. services at ''gutterman's in woodbury'' wednesday, january 31 noon.
