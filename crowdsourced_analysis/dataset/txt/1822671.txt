metro briefing | new york: queens: wounded teenager is charged   a 17-year-old who was shot on sunday when he tried to grab an off-duty police officer's pistol during a struggle in jackson heights was charged yesterday with attempted third-degree robbery, the police said. the teenager, edwin guaman, remained at elmhurst hospital center last evening, the police said. the officer, lt. jose jacome, a 16-year veteran assigned to the 110th precinct, came upon mr. guaman and another man early sunday beating a third person who was yelling that he was being robbed, the police said. lieutenant jacome identified himself as a police officer and tried to stop the robbery, the police said. it remained unclear yesterday whether the shot was accidental and who fired it, the police said.  emily vasquez
