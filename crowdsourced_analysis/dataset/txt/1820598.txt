paid notice: deaths   feigenbaum, irving leonard   feigenbaum--irving leonard., died at 85 years and 11 months. passed away, on thursday, january 18th, 2007. he left behind a wife phyllis roth-feigendaum, a stepson richard a. roth, stepdaughter charlene curtiss-roth and two grandchildren; ingrid and alexander. he did nuclear physics research at brookhaven national laboratory for 50 years. services were private.
