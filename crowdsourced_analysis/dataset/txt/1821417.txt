paid notice: deaths   stapley, edward o. dr.   stapley--edward o. dr., 79, of metuchen, nj, died on tuesday, january 23, 2007 at jfk hartwyck at oak tree in edison. he was a research microbiologist at merck & co. in rahway and madrid, spain, for 43 years retiring in 1992 as executive director of microbiology. surviving are his wife, the former helen a. strang, three daughters susan s. walko of pittsburgh, pa, robin s. goldberg of middletown, janice c. stapley of fair haven, two sisters majorie fruci of greenville, ri, jean wolosin of rockaway, nj, six grandchildren edward and victoria stapley-brown, aaron, jordan and rachel goldberg and nicholas walko. services will be saturday, january 27th, 10:00am at st. luke's church, oak ave. and middlesex ave. (rt. 27), metuchen. interment is at hillside cemetery in metuchen. visitation is friday, 79pm, at st. luke's episcopal church. services are by the costello - runyon funeral home in metuchen.
