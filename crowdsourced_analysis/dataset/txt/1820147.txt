paid notice: deaths   gooden, wilfred g., o.d.   gooden--wilfred g., o.d. 84, of riverdale, ny, died on january 6, in kingston, jamaica, of a heart attack while on a charity mission giving gifts to the children of needy families. the american friends of jamaica (afj) mourns the loss of one of its founders and an esteemed member of the board. he arrived in ny from jamaica in 1945, and through hard work and tenacity developed a construction and real estate organization. he never forgot his roots and was a devoted seventh day adventist. for 25 years he actively supported the afj with leadership and enthusiasm. he will be truly irreplaceable. all american friends of jamaica extend their heartfelt sympathy to his beloved wife of 57 years, sybil; to his brother vibert, 93, of atlanta, georgia as well as his extended family. memorial service will take place at 5:00 pm on sunday, january 21st, at the ephesus sda church at west 123rd st. and lenox ave. amb. glen a holden, ret. chair, american friends of jamaica sydney engel vice-president and treasurer
