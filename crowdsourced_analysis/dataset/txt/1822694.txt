paid notice: deaths   orange, michael m.d.   orange--michael m.d. died peacefully on january 28th at st. mary's hospital in west palm beach, fl. he is survived by his loving wife, ruth, devoted daughters, jan orange and jeri cohen, his sons'-in-law, ira podlofsky and sanford cohen, his adoring grandchildren, zachary and alexandra cohen, and jessica and david podlofsky, and his beloved brother-inlaw and sister-in-law alan and elizabeth shulman. dr. orange, a pediatrician on long island was a role model for all of his patients. he possessed a brilliant mind and had a compassionate and caring character. a member of north shore hospital since its inception, dr. orange will be missed by all those he touched. in addition to his private practice, dr. orange was a supervisor of school health for the bureau of queens in new york city. he worked diligently for the welfare of those in need in the school system and was highly regarded by the entire school health department, for his expertise. early in his career, dr. orange developed a method for determining the amount of magnesium in an infant's body. this method known as the orange method
