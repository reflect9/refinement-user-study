metro briefing | new jersey: newark: a former mayor admits bribes   joseph c. scarpelli, left, the former mayor of brick township, pleaded guilty to bribery and conspiracy charges yesterday in federal district court, officials announced. mr. scarpelli, 67, admitted that he accepted and agreed to accept cash payments from an unnamed real estate developer, said christopher j. christie, the united states attorney. mr. scarpelli said he received $5,000 in payments, mr. christie said. mr. scarpelli is free on $50,000 bail and faces 24 to 30 months in jail at sentencing, set for may 29.  john holl
