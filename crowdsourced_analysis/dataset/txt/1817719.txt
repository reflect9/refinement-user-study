paid notice: deaths  muravchik, emanuel   muravchik--emanuel. the members of the atran foundation mourn the passing of our distinguished friend and colleague manny muravchik. a foundation member representing the jewish labor committee since 1977, and director since 1984, manny was a strong, balancing force, serving with objectivity and diligence in guiding the foundation's charitable efforts. our heartfelt condolences to his wife miriam, and family. diane fischer, president
