paid notice: deaths   staudt, margaret peggy of wilton   staudt--margaret peggy of wilton, ct and point lookout, ny; formerly of larchmont, ny, died january 14th. wife of the late eugene staudt and daughter of late john and margaret ledwith. survived by eleven children, 29 grandchildren, and brother james. visitation fox funeral home in larchmont on thursday, january 18th, from 2 to 4 pm and 7 to 9 pm. funeral mass friday, january 19th, 11:00 am at st. augustine's church, larchmont. donations to st. augustine's church.
