rough rides in the big city   forty-five of the world's top professional bull riders came to manhattan this weekend for the versus invitational at madison square garden, hoping to stay atop some of the nation's top bulls in the professional bull riders tour's first visit to new york.  j. b. mauney of mooresville, n.c., last year's rookie of the year on the circuit, won the first event of his career by finishing fourth in the final round yesterday after a ride on a bull named the boogerman. he and the second-place finisher, steven shelley, were the only two riders to score on all four of their bulls this weekend.  the three-time world champion adriano moraes of brazil had won the first two rounds of competition saturday, but he was thrown by his bull in the final round yesterday and finished third over all.  bull riding
