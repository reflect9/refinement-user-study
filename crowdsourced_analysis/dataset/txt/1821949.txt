this spree must end   to the editor:  re ''a midwest nightmare, too depraved to ignore'' by pat h. broeske [jan. 14]:  here comes yet another film that exploits a murder, this time a film involving catherine keener. ms. keener has turned in some admirable work with thought-provoking scripts and will no doubt do so in the future. but surely the time has come when we can say that there is enough brutalization in our cinema, that stories of people doing awful things to each other no longer break new ground. there are stories that can examine what is worst about us without forgetting what is best. one was written by an author whom ms. keener recently played in a film: harper lee.  nick dauster  austin, tex.  'an american crime'
