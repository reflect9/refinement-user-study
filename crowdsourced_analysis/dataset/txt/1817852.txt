arts, briefly; san francisco opera names music director   nicola luisotti, an up-and-coming italian conductor, will become the new music director of the san francisco opera, the company said yesterday. mr. luisotti, 45, will assume the job for the 2009-10 season. he will take over for donald runnicles, whose contract expires the previous season. mr. luisotti, who early in his career was a staff conductor at the teatro alla scala in milan, recently made debuts with the metropolitan opera, seattle opera and los angeles opera.  daniel j. wakin
