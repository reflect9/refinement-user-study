metro briefing | new york: yonkers: city agrees to bronx river cleanup plan   the city of yonkers has agreed to end its four-year opposition to a state plan to clean up the city's discharge of sewage into the bronx river, attorney general andrew m. cuomo announced yesterday. the settlement, approved earlier this month by a state supreme court justice in westchester county, calls for the city to pay $2.2 million for a study to identify sources of pollution and then to promptly clean up the sites identified by the study.
