paid notice: deaths  laudenslager, pauline a.   laudenslager -- pauline a., on january 5, 2007, in new york city. wife of richard l. laudenslager of worcester, pa (d. 1950), daughter of minnie and austin fleming, she was born in elk garden, west virginia. she graduated from barnard college, nyc, in 1940. her m.a. from northeastern illinois university led to a life dedicated to her special education students in chicago and baltimore. a long time supporter of the arts, she is survived by one daughter, carol ann, aka carolyn lord, of nyc. she will join richard at hershey cemetery, pa.
