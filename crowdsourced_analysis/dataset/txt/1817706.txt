paid notice: deaths   friedman, martha   friedman--martha, died on wednesday january 3rd, 2007, at mt. sinai medical center in nyc at the age of 91. the cause of death was a heart attack according to her daughter, dr. joann urquhart. dr. friedman was a psychotherapist with a private practice which she maintained until she was 90 years old, and was also a published author. she was an assistant professor of psychiatry at new york medical college and new york hospital, and taught the course ''overcoming the fear of success'' at the new school in new york. that course formed the basis of her 1980 book of the same title. she was born on august 15th, 1916, in new york city, the second daughter of paul and molly schwabinger. she graduated from the college of william and mary, and also earned a doctorate from columbia university. over the years martha was active in many social causes with her beloved husband of 40 years, julius friedman, who died in 1982. her sister ruth charney died in 1998. she is survived by her beloved children and their spouses michael and linda friedman, joann and michael urquhart, and peter and robin friedman; and her adored grandchildren elizabeth, will, max, joe, and steve. a memorial service will be held february 24, from 4-6pm at the community church of new york, 40 east 35th st, ny, ny 10016. in lieu of flowers please make a contribution to the american heart association, 122 east 42nd st, ny, ny 10168.
