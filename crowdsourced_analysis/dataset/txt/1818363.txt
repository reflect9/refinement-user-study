streep to star in film version of abba musical   meryl streep will take on the role of a mother with a rebel streak when she stars in the film version of the international hit musical ''mamma mia!,'' reuters reported.  the film will be the first musical for ms. streep, who has, nevertheless, sung in the movies ''a prairie home companion,'' ''postcards from the edge'' and ''death becomes her.''  the story of ''mamma mia!'' features 22 abba songs and revolves around a bride-to-be and her once-rebellious single mother, who reared her on a greek island without disclosing the identity of her child's father. the young woman locates three possibilities and invites them to the wedding.
