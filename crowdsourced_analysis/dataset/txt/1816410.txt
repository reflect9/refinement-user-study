paid notice: deaths   fisch, herbert j., m.d.   fisch--herbert j., m.d., died january 3rd, 2007, after a courageous battle with melanoma. survived by his beloved wife elinor, proud and loving children jonathan fisch, amy and jacob wasserman and peter and jill fisch, and adoring grandchildren sam, jessica, ben, emily, daniel, zach, sara and matthew. outstanding physician for 46 years, 27 years as the chief of anesthesiology at peninsula hospital center, president of the new york state society of anesthesiologists in 1992. world war ii navy veteran. scholar, a man of great integrity, photographer, historian and model shipbuilder. he will always be with us.  fisch--dr. herbert. the partners, counsel, associates and staff of the firm of paul, weiss, rifkind, wharton & garrison llp express profound sorrow at the death on january 3rd of dr. herbert fisch, beloved father of our friend and partner, peter e. fisch. we express our deepest sympathies to peter and to all other members of the family.
