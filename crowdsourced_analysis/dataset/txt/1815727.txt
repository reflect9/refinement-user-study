paid notice: deaths   long, william albers of potomac, md   long--william albers of potomac, md, died peacefully wednesday night in his home, surrounded by his entire family. bill is remembered as a managing partner of latham and watkins' washington office, a deputy under secretary of defense during the regan administration, a managing director of the carlyle group, an inveterate member of burning tree, and a loving friend and family member. he is survived by his wife janey long; children pauliann marie long, augustine joseph long, edward lawrence long, christopher william long, michelle long langemeier, and sean coakley long; and siblings patricia long bonn, nancy long hogan, augustine anthony long, paul joseph long, katherine long wolf, and clare long martin. he counted grandchildren in the double-digits and reached a low handicap in the single digits. friends will be received at pumphreys bethesda-chevy chase funeral home 7557 wisconsin ave., bethesda, md on tuesday, january 2, 2007 from 7-9 pm. memorial mass will be offered at st. bartholomews catholic church, 6900 river road, bethesda, md on wednesday, january 3, 2007 at 10:00 am. in lieu of flowers, friends are encouraged to send donations in the name of william a. long to the inova fairfax hospital lung fund, 3300 gallows road, falls church, va 22042, attn: debbie campbell transport center. for information: robert a. pumphrey funeral home bethesdachevy chase.
