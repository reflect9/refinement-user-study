paid notice: deaths   matthews, helen thompson   matthews--helen thompson of wellesley, ma died thursday, january 4, 2007. born in bath, maine on september 19, 1917, she was the daughter of helen and harold thompson. she graduated from brunswick high school in maine and wellesley college, class of 1939. she resided in port washington, new york for over 45 years with her late husband, william t. matthews and more recently returned to being a resident of wellesley where she resided with her grandson, william t. matthews, ii and his wife, deborah katz. grandmother, role model, friend, scrabble champion and beloved -she will be truly missed.
