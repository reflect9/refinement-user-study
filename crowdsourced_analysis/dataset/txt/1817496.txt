paid notice: deaths   sands, harry, ph.d.   sands--harry, ph.d. the members of the new york state psychological association are saddened by the death of our respected and beloved colleague and past president (1985-1986), harry sands. harry was an acclaimed psychologist, winner of the apa's gold medal for lifetime achievement, and a friend and counselor to the world. he first joined nyspa in 1952. our condolences to harry's loving wife, helene, and his family.
