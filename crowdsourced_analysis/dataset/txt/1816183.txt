metro briefing | new york: queens: armed robbery suspects sought   the police are seeking three men suspected of being involved in six armed robberies in queens last month. the police said two or three armed men robbed a storage center, a parking garage, two auto repair shops, a commercial building and a store. in the last two robberies, on dec. 20 and 26, employees were locked in an office after the holdup, the police said. the men are believed to be in their 20s. one had worn a blue bandanna and another a hooded sweatshirt, the police said.
