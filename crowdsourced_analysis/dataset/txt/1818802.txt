rebekah dopp, theodore calabrese   rebekah evangeline dopp is to be married today to theodore steven calabrese in the chapel of the hackley school in tarrytown, n.y. the rev. leslie harry cohen, an interfaith minister, is to officiate.  the bride, 30, is a director of affiliate relations at hbo in new york. she graduated from the college of william and mary.  she is a daughter of stephanie evangeline dopp and charles g. dopp of middletown, del. her father works in philadelphia as a vice president for programming of ''comcast newsmakers,'' an interview series produced by comcast, the cable television company.  the bridegroom, 28, is a director of business development in new york at computershare, an australian firm that serves the securities industry. he graduated from gettysburg college and received a law degree from new york law school.  he is the son of patricia carr calabrese and steven calabrese of irvington, n.y. his mother is a sales agent at country village real estate in nantucket, mass. his father works in new york and shelton, conn., managing the medicare programs of health net northeast, the managed-care insurance company.  weddings/celebrations
