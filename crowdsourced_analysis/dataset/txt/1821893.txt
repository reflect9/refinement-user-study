paid notice: deaths   ziskind, samuel   ziskind--samuel, died peacefully on january 10. beloved brother of muriel k. harsip; devoted uncle of herbert harsip of newton, ma, janice hurwitz of framingham, ma, and barry harsip of boxborough, ma. cherished friend of ed strum. a memorial service will be held on sunday, february 18, at the village temple, 33 east 12th street, new york city at 12:00 noon. sam was a unique and gentle combination of intelligence and concern with an insatiable quest for learning and understanding.
