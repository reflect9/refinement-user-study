alexis chiang, thomas colvin   dr. alexis shih-wen chiang, a daughter of ning chiang and dr. george c. chiang of wilmington, del., was married yesterday afternoon to thomas orlin colvin, the son of ava maria colvin and judge john o. colvin of arlington, va. the bridegroom's father, who is the chief judge of the united states tax court, officiated at the princeton university chapel.  the bride, 30, will be known as dr. colvin. she is a chief resident in orthopedic surgery at new york university hospital for joint diseases. she graduated from princeton and received her medical degree from the mount sinai school of medicine in new york.  her father is a business consultant on buying and selling chemicals. her mother is a senior database administrator at wilmington trust, a bank.  the bridegroom, who is 31 and also a princeton graduate, is a second-year m.b.a. student at new york university.  his mother retired as a librarian at zachary taylor elementary school in arlington.  weddings/celebrations  correction:  january 21, 2007, sunday  a report on jan. 7, about the chiang-colvin wedding, misstated the bridegroom's given name. he is timothy colvin, not thomas.
