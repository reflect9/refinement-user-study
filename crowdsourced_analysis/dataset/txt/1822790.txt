you won. now legislate!   to the editor:  frank rich (column, jan. 28) got it right: who in washington will be brave enough to act?  i suggest that the presidential contenders for 2008, both democrats and republicans, do what they were sent to washington to do -- legislate.  waiting for a new president to stop the bloodletting is not what the people mandated in the 2006 elections.  fletcher copp  new york, jan. 28, 2007
