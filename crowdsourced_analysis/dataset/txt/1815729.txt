paid notice: deaths   manheimer, robert h., m.d.   manheimer--robert h., m.d. died after a short illness at the age of 88 on december 31, 2006. he was a dedicated rheumatologist, who worked at montefiore hospital for nearly sixty years. he is predeceased by his wife may rosner manheimer and survived and greatly missed by his sons and their families: dr. eric manheimer, his wife diana taylor, and their children alexei and marina; dean manheimer, his wife keri, and their children elizabeth and holly; josh manheimer, his wife renee, and their children tomas, alejandra, and mia, and by his partner of 12 years, blossom krakauer. services being held on tuesday, january 2nd at 11:15 in the morning, at the riverside on 76th street and amsterdam avenue. the family will receive guests for shiva on wednesday, january 3rd at dr. eric manheimer's residence.
