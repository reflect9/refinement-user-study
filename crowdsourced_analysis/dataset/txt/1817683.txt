metro briefing | new jersey: newark: hockey arena to be named for prudential   newark's new hockey arena will be called the prudential center under an agreement announced yesterday by the new jersey devils and prudential financial inc., the newark-based financial services company. prudential will pay about $100 million over 20 years under the terms of the agreement. the 18,000-seat, $375 million arena is scheduled to open in the fall.  ronald smothers
