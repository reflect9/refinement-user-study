paid notice: deaths   rowen, dr. ruth halle   rowen--dr. ruth halle. born 1918, professor emeritus at cuny, passed away on january 5, following a severe stroke. one of the first graduates (1948) of the doctoral program of musicology at columbia university, she wrote several books, designed the graduate program in musicology at ccny, and was a founding member of the doctoral faculty at cuny. she is survived by a loving son and daughter, four grandchildren, and four greatgrandchildren.
