paid notice: deaths   marchand, lucienne   marchand--lucienne died december 28. she was 98. born olga lucienne espir in paris, she spent the last 66 years living in new york. wife of the late max marchand, she is survived by sons and daughters-in-law lucien simon and phyllis marchand of princeton, nj and daniel and brenda marchand of norwood, ma; four grandchildren; and six great-grandchildren. memorial contributions may be made to the jewish center, 435 nassau street, princeton, nj 08540.
