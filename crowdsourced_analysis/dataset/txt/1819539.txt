webb to run wanamaker   alan webb, the united states' most successful young middle-distance runner, will compete in three eastern indoor meets the next three weekends. webb had previously committed to the mile races in the new balance games on saturday at the armory in upper manhattan and the reebok boston indoor games on jan. 27. millrose games officials said yesterday that webb would run on feb. 2 in their wanamaker mile at madison square garden.  frank litsky  sports briefing: track and field
