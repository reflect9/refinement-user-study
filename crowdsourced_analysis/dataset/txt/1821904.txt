clockwise, everywhere   to the editor: ''punta del este, uruguay; feeling at home among the elite'' by matt gross (frugal traveler, jan. 7), repeats an age-old urban legend: that water spins down a drain counterclockwise in the southern hemisphere: ''i could hear the money running merrily down the drain -- counterclockwise, of course: this was the southern hemisphere.''  there's just one problem: it ain't so.  for a full debunking of this alleged effect, please see snopes.com/science/coriolis.asp.  glenn weinstein  marietta, ga.
