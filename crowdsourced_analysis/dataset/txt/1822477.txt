paid notice: deaths   edwards, alfred l.   edwards--alfred l. age 86, of ann arbor died january 26, 2007. funeral services held friday, february 2, 2007. in 1963 he was appointed by president kennedy to become the deputy assistant secretary of agriculture. he was the first african american to hold that high a position in the agriculture department. he received the distinguished service award in the department in 1969. services with muehlig funeral chapel, ann arbor, mi.
