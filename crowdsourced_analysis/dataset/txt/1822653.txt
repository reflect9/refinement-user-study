corrections   an article on friday about groups that planned an antiwar march in washington on saturday misstated the prominence of one labor group, the service employees international union, which provides organizational and financial support for the group americans against escalation in iraq. the service employees union, which says it has 1.8 million members in the united states and canada, is not the largest labor organization in the country; the national education association, which describes itself as a professional employee association that represents 3.2 million teachers, is the biggest.
