espn cancels talk show   espn yesterday canceled stephen a. smith's nightly series, ''quite frankly,'' which had suffered from low viewership since its inception in august 2005. the final episode of the talk show, which also featured smith providing commentary to a studio audience, was to be shown last night.  the program, in its 11 p.m. time slot, attracted an average of 240,000 households, but the figure has been as low as 196,000.  smith will be seen more often on ''sportscenter'' and on espn's national basketball association programming, and will be host of four interview specials.  richard sandomir  sports briefing: tv sports
