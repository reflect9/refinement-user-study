beach access and property taxes   to the editor:  re ''broadening beach access'' (editorial, dec. 10): you describe some residents of long beach island as ''vehemently opposed'' to proposed regulations for beach access. i am one of those residents, and my opposition is based on how these regulations would affect property taxes.  the accessways, restrooms and parking mandated by the state would strain local coffers. any progress legislators made in reducing taxes would be offset by executive branch regulations.  mary derogatis  long beach township  the writer is an officer of save lbi beaches advocacy group.
