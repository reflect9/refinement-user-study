paid notice: deaths   walsh, annmarie   walsh--annmarie, died january 11th, 2007. survived by husband john f. walsh, her son peter, her daughter-inlaw kimberly, her grandchildren, oscar, eloise, her sister jean h. wiley, her niece tricia deans. her children caroline, james, and david predeceased her. annmarie graduated from the madeira school and barnard college, and received her ph.d. from columbia. among other things she had been a professor at the graduate school of cuny and president of and subsequently the luther gulick distinguished scholar in residence at the institute of public administration in ny. annmarie sat on various boards and commissions, including the national academy of public administration. in addition to articles and studies on wide-ranging topics, she authored a number of books on public administration and urban planning, such as: the public's business, a study of public authority; the urban challenge to government, an international comparison of 13 cities; urban government for the paris region; co-authored urban government for metropolitan lagos; co-authored urban government for zagreb yugoslavia; and co-edited agenda for a city, issues confronting ny. a service will be held at blessed sacrament church. 152 w. 71st st., ny, 11am, monday, january 15th. in lieu of flowers, donations to the david walsh fund at banks street school for children 610 west 112th street, ny, ny 10025, would be appreciated.
