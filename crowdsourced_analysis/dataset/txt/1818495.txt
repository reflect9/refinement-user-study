corrections   a chart on jan. 2 with the continuation of a front-page article about wal-mart's struggle to market an energy-saving light bulb known as a compact fluorescent lamp referred incompletely to mercury emissions in comparing two light bulbs. the emissions -- 2.4 milligrams from the fluorescent lamp and 10 milligrams for an incandescent bulb -- occur at a coal-powered plant generating the electricity needed for the bulbs. the bulbs themselves do not emit mercury.
