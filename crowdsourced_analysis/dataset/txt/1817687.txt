metro briefing | new jersey: newark: youth worker charged with abuse   investigators for the essex county prosecutor have arrested an irvington man who worked for a youth program financed by the state and charged him with molesting an 11-year-old girl whose family he befriended. the man, lawrence tyler, 56, worked for the youth advocacy program, a private agency that has a state contract. he was arrested at his home late friday night and charged with five counts of sexual assault and five counts of endangering the welfare of a child, said mark ali, director of the county's child abuse unit. mr. tyler had been working for the girl's uncle, mr. ali said.  john holl
