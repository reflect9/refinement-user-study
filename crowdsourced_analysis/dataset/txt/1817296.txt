at colleges, tradition counts   to the sports editor:  re ''with a game at dartmouth, fighting sioux stir debate,'' dec. 29: with all due respect to the dartmouth athletic director and the national collegiate athletic association, and their sensitivity toward the use of american indian names, mascots and imagery, has political correctness gotten out of hand? history and tradition should not continue to be dismissed as inconsequential.  james r. spence jr.  williamsburg, va.
