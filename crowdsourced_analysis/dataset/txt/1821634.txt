paid notice: deaths   klein, sadie   klein--sadie, died peacefully at home on january 24, 2008 at age 101. devoted wife of emmanuel (deceased). cherished mother of norma and victor (both deceased) and mother-in-law of dorothy. beloved grandmother of jennifer, katie, and emily. adoring great-grandmother of ezekiel. a passionate activist, a lover of literature, a ferocious player of tennis and anagrams, a witty correspondent, sadie will be deeply missed by her family and her many friends of all ages. a memorial reception will be held at her apartment from 59pm on friday, february 1.
