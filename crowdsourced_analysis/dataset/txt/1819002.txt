paid notice: deaths   kaufman, marilyn   kaufman--marilyn, 80, of marlboro, nj, died january 14, 2007 at home. born and raised in brooklyn, ny, mrs. kaufman lived in goshen, ny for eight years before moving to marlboro six years ago. she was the devoted wife of joseph i. kaufman, who died in 2004. they had been married for 54 years. surviving are a son and daughter-inlaw, mitchell and lucy kaufman of marlboro; a daughter, saundra marc of manalapan; four grandchildren, scott kaufman, jeremy kaufman, samantha marc and zachary marc; and a sister, constance levy of delray beach, fl. funeral services will be held january 15, 2007 at 11am, bloomfield cooper jewish chapels, 44 wilson ave, manalapan, nj. internment to follow at beth david cemetery, elmont, ny. memorial contributions may be made to the cancer institute of new jersey, 195 little albany st., new brunswick, nj 08903
