paid notice: deaths   rossi, douglas a.   rossi--douglas a., 1953-2008, ended his battle with cancer after 5 years on tuesday, january 22. a scuba diver, sailor, hiker, kayaker, traveler, builder and writer, doug lived every one of his 54 years to the fullest. he will be remembered for his wit, his unique perspective on life and as a true boatswain mate. his family will miss him dearly.
