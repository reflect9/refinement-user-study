paid notice: deaths   mceniry, murray w.   mceniry--murray w., 92, former general counsel to mobil oil corporation's international division and a resident of scarsdale for more than 50 years, died saturday, january 13, in cambridge, massachusetts. he was born july 2, 1914 in bessemer, alabama, the son of william hugh and dora winters mceniry. after attending bessemer public schools he was graduated from birminghamsouthern college in 1936 and columbia law school in 1939. drafted in march 1941, he served nearly five years in the army, rising to major and receiving the bronze star medal for meritorious service. on december 21, 1946, he married catherine mcpolan of dallas, texas, to whom he was married for 60 years. he is survived by his wife; by their daughter catherine and her husband david sullivan; by their three sons: philip mceniry; dr. david mceniry and his wife dr. carolyn acosta; roger mceniry and his wife courtney mceniry; and byseven grandchildren: john, thomas, and peter sullivan and tessa, andrew, grace, and eloise mceniry. he was predeceased by his brothers, william hugh mceniry, jr. and ralph mceniry. memorial private. contributions in his memory may be made to hitchcock presbyterian church, 6 greenacres avenue, scarsdale, or any charity of the donor's choice.
