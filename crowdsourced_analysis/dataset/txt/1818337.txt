world briefing | americas: mexico: troops to acapulco   president felipe calderón continued a crackdown on drug cartels and sent more than 1,000 soldiers into the pacific resort town of acapulco and other areas of guerrero state. the deployments follow similar operations in the states of michoacán and baja california involving 10,000 troops and agents since mr. calderón took office in december.  james c. mckinley jr.
