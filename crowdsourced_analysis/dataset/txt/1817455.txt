metro briefing | new jersey: newark: robber is sentenced   a 19-year-old man, who is awaiting trial on charges of killing an orange police officer last august, was sentenced to 14 years in prison on friday for his role in an armed robbery that occurred before the officer's killing, the authorities said. the man, raynard brown of orange, pleaded guilty to robbery and weapon possession last year for his role in robbing a 34-year-old woman in orange and stealing her cellphone. he is accused of fatally shooting detective kieran t. shields on aug. 7. he has pleaded not guilty.  john holl
