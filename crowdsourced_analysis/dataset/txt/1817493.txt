paid notice: deaths   rosenfield, helen anna   rosenfield--helen anna, of brooklyn, new york, died peacefully on sunday, january 7, 2007 at the age of 95 . she was predeceased by her husband harry in 1969. she is survived by her son stephen rosenfield and his wife riva rosenfield, her daughter mimi meyers and husband, gary mangus, her four grandchildren, peter rosenfield, jonathan rosenfield, joan meyers, and gregory meyers, and her two greatgrandchildren, hannah rosenfield and jessie rosenfield. funeral services will be held at 12 pm on wednesday, january 10, 2007 at the weinstein - garlick - kirshenbau chapels, 1153 coney island ave., brooklyn, ny, 11230. active in progressive political causes, helen was a spirited and compassionate woman who will be missed by her family and friends. in lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the aclu foundation, 125 broad street, new york, ny, 10004.
