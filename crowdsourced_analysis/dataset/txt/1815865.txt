paid notice: deaths   greene, shirley k.   greene--shirley k., age 88, of new york and connecticut. loving wife to the late myron greene, died december 25th, 2006. devoted mother, grandmother, great grandmother, and sister. graduate of hunter college and yale, worked initially in the public health field and then with husband myron for over 50 years. always available to her family, nieces, and nephews for counsel, support, and comfort. she was laid to rest at westchester hills cemetery on december 29th. she was greatly loved and will be deeply missed.
