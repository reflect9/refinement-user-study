metro briefing | new york: manhattan: hells angels member says he'll sue city   richard west, a member of the new york chapter of the hells angels, says he will file a lawsuit against the city, claiming that he was illegally taken into custody after a search of the motorcycle club's east village headquarters at 77 east third street on monday. dozens of police officers, backed by sharpshooters, helicopters and heavy equipment, used a search warrant to seek evidence connected to the beating of a woman outside the building on sunday night. ronald l. kuby, a lawyer for the hells angels, said the police used excessive force. the club had refused to allow several officers to enter the heavily fortified building, so the larger contingent arrived.  thomas j. lueck
