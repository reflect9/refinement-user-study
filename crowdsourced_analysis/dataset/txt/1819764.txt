paid notice: deaths   berry, dorothy m.   berry--dorothy m., 87, died january 14th 2007, widow of dr. edgar berry. she was a retired navy lieutenant, 1941 radcliffe graduate, past president of the lenox hill hospital women's auxiliary and past president of the new york state a.m.a. auxiliary. she is survived by two children, dr. william berry of portsmouth, nh and dorothy plessner of ozona, fl and five grandchildren. in lieu of flowers donations should be made to the lenox hill women's auxiliary.  $(6$)berry--dorothy. the auxiliary of lenox hill hospital notes with deep sadness the passing of dorothy berry. a valued member for 45 years, she will be greatly missed by all whose lives she touched.
