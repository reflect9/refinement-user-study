paid notice: deaths   olcott, bernard   olcott--bernard, 88, of weehawken, nj and southampton, ny, on december 30, 2006. devoted father to blair de zagon, james olcott and victoria olcott; loving grandfather to celine and grant olcott, ava and alexandre de zagon. as an inventor, electrical engineer and patent attorney, mr. olcott founded, and tirelessly served as ceo to olcott international and company, a pioneer and global leader in the patent annuity payment and trademark renewal industry, serving the intellectual property profession. mr. olcott served as a first lieutenant in the us army during wwii, and was a member of the new york yacht club. a memorial service will be held on january 25, 2007 at 11:00 a.m. at the william j. leber funeral home, 2000 kennedy blvd., union city, nj. in lieu of flowers, please make a donation in bernard's memory to a charity of your choice.
