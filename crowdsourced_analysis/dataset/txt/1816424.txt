paid notice: deaths   schneller, leo, m.d.   schneller--leo, m.d., age 91 of new rochelle, ny died january 3, 2007. adored husband of the late thelma (nee rosenberg). loving father of david (marilynn), margery and julie. beloved grandfather of david, lisa and michael. cherished uncle of john (ruth), nancy (jim), laurie (howie), tom (anna), and their children. a graduate of the university of vermont medical school, former clinical director of adult psychiatry at westchester county medical center, and associate professor of clinical psychiatry at new york medical college. he was a physician respected by all. truly his greatest success was his loving devotion to his family which will be forever in our hearts. graveside service sunday january 7 at 11am at sharon gardens cemetery, valhalla, ny.
