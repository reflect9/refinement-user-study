paid notice: deaths   liberman, pearl   liberman--pearl, 91, of bal harbour, florida, died on january 9, 2007. she was the beloved wife of the late dr. george liberman; loving mother of ellen goldschild (david) and david liberman (marie); cherished grandmother of michael and noah; dear sister of morton (robin) cooper; the late sidney (florence) cooper, blanche beckmann, anita fishbein and sister-in-law of sue liberman. she is survived by countless family and friends who will miss her dearly. she supported numerous charitable and civic organizations. past president of the women's auxiliary, kings county, she also served on the executive boards of the brooklyn association for mental health; the american cancer society, new york division; the brooklyn arthritis foundation; medgar evers college; and the brooklyn institute of art and sciences. she is the past president of the board of trustees, brooklyn academy of music, and was a trustee of the brooklyn museum of art. she was recipient of the national council of christians and jews award and the medgar evers college award for community relations service. she was a humanitarian of the society of mount sinai and a founder of the mount sinai medical center. she was a member of citizens interested in arts, inc. in lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the brooklyn museum of art or the mount sinai medical center foundation, miami beach, in favor of the wien center. services will be held on sunday at 11:15 am at ''the riverside'' 76th street at amsterdam.
