paid notice: deaths   ruggles, ruth cain   ruggles--ruth cain. mrs. ruggles, age 93, of lost tree village, north palm beach, florida passed away peacefully at home on sunday, january 7, 2007. she was the daughter of the late rolly m. cain and dercy myers cain, and resided in wilmette, illinois before moving to palm beach. mrs. ruggles was predeceased by her husband of 54 years, rudy l. ruggles in 1992, and a daughter, jean ruggles romoser in 1989. mrs. ruggles is survived by her fiance, richard l. williams of palm beach; a son, rudy l. ruggles, jr. of ridgefield, connecticut; six grandchildren: leslie rylee, rudy l. ruggles, iii, christopher ruggles, whitney savignano, daniel ruggles and andrew ruggles; and six great-grandchildren: lindsey ruggles, thomas ruggles, michela savignano, preston rylee, costas savignano and ava rylee. a memorial service will take place at lost tree chapel, 11149 turtle beach road, lost tree village on saturday, january 13th at 11:00am. in lieu of flowers, please direct contributions in memory of mrs. ruggles to hospice of palm beach county, 5300 east avenue, west palm beach, fl 33407.
