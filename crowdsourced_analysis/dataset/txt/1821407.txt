paid notice: deaths   kirkland, irena   kirkland--irena. the board of directors, overseers and staff of the internatinal rescue committee are saddened by the death of irena kirkland, a valued member of our board since 1981. mrs. kirkland, who was herself a refugee from czechloslovakia, and a survivor of a nazi concentration camp, made significant contributions to the cause of refugees and the work of the irc. she believed deeply in freedom, democracy, and human rights, and strongly supported the efforts of dissident movements in eastern europe during the cold war. we will miss her deeply, and we extend our heartfelt sympathy to her sister, mrs. alena lourie, and her family. alan batkin and jonathan wiesner, co-chairmen of the board tom brokaw and winston lord, co-chairmen, irc overseers george rupp, president  kirkland--irena. on wednesday, january 24, 2007, irena kirkland of washington, dc. beloved wife of the late lane kirland, devoted sister of alena lourie, loving aunt of jonathan lourie. also survived by great-nieces, kinneret, irit, shlomit, amanda; and a great-nephew nachum. inurnment services will be held on monday, january 29 at 1pm, at arlington national cemetery, arlington, va. procession to form from administration building. memorial contributions may be made to the international rescue committee or the national endowment for democracy. services entrusted to edward sagel funeral direction, rockville, md.
