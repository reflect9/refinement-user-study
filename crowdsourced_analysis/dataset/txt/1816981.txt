art vs. administration   to the editor:  re ''he parked his barre in harvard yard'' by daniel j. wakin [dec. 31]:  i congratulate the dancer damian woetzel on pursuing a master's in public administration at harvard. but while studying to become a successful artistic director of a major ballet company, will he explore the humanities to interpret dances of the past and nurture new repertory? here's hoping that the education of damian woetzel, whose development as an artist, scholar and administrator matters, doesn't stop at the glass door of the business suite.  jeff friedman  woodbridge, n.j.  the writer teaches dance history at rutgers university.  damian woetzel
