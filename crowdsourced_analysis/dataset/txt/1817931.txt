paid notice: deaths   murphy, catherine farrell   murphy--catherine farrell, of new york, ny, died on tuesday, january 9, 2007, in boca raton, fl, with her beloved husband, bill, at her side. she was 77. she is survived by her husband, william debar murphy, her daughter, susan murphy ferrand, and her son, anthony murphy; five grandchildren, hannah, matilda, oliver, samuel and aidan; two sisters, eileen french and jean roth, and two brothers, francis farrell and martin farrell; and scores of beloved nieces, nephews and friends. a memorial service will be held in new york in february.
