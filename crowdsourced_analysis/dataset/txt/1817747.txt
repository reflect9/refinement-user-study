mayonnaise acquires an accent   stokes mayonnaise dressings, newcomers from england, are so rich they could be mistaken for homemade. they are made with organic eggs and canola oil, with extra virgin olive oil in some of them as well. the blushed tomato flavor is excellent for sandwiches and cold seafood. curried coronation sauce, with bits of raisins and apricots, will do wonders for hard-cooked eggs and chicken salad. serve the mustard and honey flavor with meats like cold roast pork. there is also an herb variety as well as a plain one.  chelsea market baskets in the chelsea market sells them for $6.95 for about 12 ounces, and they can be ordered at chelseamarketbaskets.com.  food stuff
