world business briefing | asia: hong kong: new editor at south china morning post   the south china morning post, the largest english-language daily newspaper in hong kong, said the paper's editor, fanny fung, and managing editor, pauline loong, had resigned, the latest in a series of personnel changes over the last year. the resignations, disclosed in an internal memo late last week by kuok khoon ean, chairman of the post group, came shortly after that of the business editor, stuart jackson. c. k. lau was named editor; mark clifford remains editor in chief.  patrick l. smith
