devon beddard, scott caraher   devon elizabeth beddard, the daughter of michael j. beddard of watch hill, r.i., and the late anita p. beddard, was married yesterday to scott clinton caraher, a son of judith k. caraher and james c. caraher of chicago. the rev. philippa a. turner, an episcopal priest, performed the ceremony at the church of the heavenly rest in new york.  mrs. caraher, 28, is the promotions manager at tiffany & company in new york. she graduated from trinity college in hartford. her father, who is retired, was a vice president and a stockbroker at smith barney in new york.  mr. caraher, 30, is an associate portfolio manager working in new york and san francisco for symphony asset management, a hedge fund unit of nuveen instruments. he graduated from georgetown. his parents own windsor outdoor, a billboard company in chicago. the bridegroom's mother is also an assistant headmistress and a teacher at st. chrysostom's day school, a preschool in chicago.  weddings/celebrations
