paid notice: deaths   sands, harry, ph.d.   sands--harry, ph.d., the postgraduate center for mental health and the board of trustees mourn the loss of our long time friend, board member and former executive director. harry established our first community residence and set the course for our future. he leaves a legacy of commitment to serving the needs of people with psychiatric disabilities as well as supporting the training of mental health professionals. we extend our condolences to his wife helene and the entire family. jacob barak, ph.d., president and ceo allan goldberg, chairman of the board of trustees
