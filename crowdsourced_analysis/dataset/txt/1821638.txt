paid notice: deaths   leonidas, john c., m.d.   leonidas--john c., m.d., died on january 24, 2008 after struggling with parkinson's disease and myelodysplastic syndrome. born in athens in 1930, dr. leonidas left greece in 1960 to train in pediatric radiology at babies hospital, columbia presbyterian medical center. he was later professor and chief of pediatric radiology at mount sinai medical center in new york city, children's mercy hospital in kansas city, and tufts new england medical center in boston. he recently retired as professor and chief of pediatric radiology at schneider children's hospital, long island jewish medical center. dr. leonidas was a brilliant and world renowned pediatric radiologist who authored over 150 journal articles and gave over 90 presentations throughout the world. a celebrated teacher, he inspired many students to become pediatric radiologists. dr. leonidas was president and chairman of the board of the society for pediatric radiology, which honored him with its gold medal in 2006. he is survived by his three sons, eric, cary and darren, and by his cherished grandchildren, elenore and jonathan. he is also survived by his sister, olga economou, and by his nephew, andonis economou, of athens, greece. a memorial service will be held monday, january 28th at 3:30pm at frank e. campbell funeral chapel, 1076 madison ave. the family requests that in lieu of flowers and gifts donations be made to the michael j. fox foundation for parkinson's research or the nyu parkinson's and movement disorder center.
