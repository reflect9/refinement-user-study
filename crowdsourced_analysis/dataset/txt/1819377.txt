the new place, which is to open tomorrow in soho, is familiar, too, with dishes like baby octopus, potatoes and black olives or branzino with brussels sprouts and tomato confit. the panini and salads echo the offerings of his falai panetteria. the new place will be open from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m. customers can bring their own wine and beer. dinner hours will be extended in the spring: 265 lafayette street (prince street), (917) 338-6207.  havana central at the west end -- the finishing touches are just about done on the $2.5 million renovation of the west end, the venerable morningside heights
