design site prefers the old to the new   internet sites devoted to design seem to multiply by the day, most of them focused on the same new products. barbara pershyn-davis, a decorator in cherry valley, n.y., takes a different tack: her new web site, eye-to-eyemagazine.com, photographed and written by her, focuses on beautiful images of old rooms.  the first installment, posted on dec. 26, contains nearly 100 images of poetically tattered rooms from artfully rustic locations near her upstate home. she plans to show places in new york and london in the coming months. ''eye-to-eye is more about an aesthetic than about things,'' she said. ''i don't want it to be a buying magazine.''  michael cannell  currents: on the web
