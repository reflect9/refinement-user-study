paid notice: deaths   breck, rachel hammond   breck--rachel hammond, on january 1, 2007, aged 98. a cattle breeder and for many years owner of hedgerows farm, west cornwall, ct, she was sometimes referred to as ''first lady of the angus industry'' and was inducted into the aberdeen angus hall of fame. she is survived by her daughter leith speiden, by her grandchildren, terry mcclenahan, barbara mcclenahan shephard, adam klauber, blake klauber, rachel klauber speiden, and by five great-grandchildren. services on saturday, january 6, at 2 pm at christ church episcopal, sharon, ct. in lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to miss porter's school, farmington, ct.
