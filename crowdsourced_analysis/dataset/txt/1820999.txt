paid notice: deaths  shneidman, dr. conalee   shneidman--dr. conalee. congregation rodeph sholom mourns the death of our beloved, long-time member, connie shneidman, wife of dr. lee shneidman and mother of philip and jack. as our first outreach chairperson, hers was a warm and welcoming voice to those seeking jewish community. our deepest sympathy is extended to all their dear ones. robert n levine, dd, senior rabbi roger meltzer president  $(6$)shneidman--conalee (nee levine), died suddenly on monday january 22nd. beloved wife for 45 years of j. lee shneidman, mother of philip and jack, and sister-inlaw to daniel and doris shneidman. dr. conalee levine-shneidman, a former associate professor in the clinical psychology program at nyu, was a psychoanalyst in private practice for 45 years. a lifelong new yorker, she enjoyed partaking in the city's museums, theaters, concert halls and thrift shops. connie shneidman attended black mountain college, and received her ph.d and postdoctorate certificate from nyu. member of apa, the new york state psychological association, director of crisis intervention center at nyu, coordinator for emergency services-nyspa/red cross. author of ''too smart for her own good?'' and coauthor of numerous psychohistorical articles with her husband, a professor of history. member of the executive committee and training analyst at the object relations institute. aside from her immediate family she leaves behind 21 first cousins, seven nieces and nephews, and five grand-nieces and grand-nephews. services will be friday january 26th at 11:15am at ''the riverside'' 76th st. and amsterdam ave.
