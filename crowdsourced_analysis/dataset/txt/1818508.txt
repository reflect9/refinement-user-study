highway walker struck by 2 cars   a man walking along a westbound lane of the grand central parkway near la guardia airport last night was hit by two cars and killed, the police said.  just before 8 p.m., the pedestrian, a 55-year-old hispanic man whose identity was not released by the police, was struck by a green dodge neon driven by a 30-year-old man, then by a white honda driven by a 31-year-old woman, the police said.  the first driver remained at the scene until the police arrived, they said, but the second, unaware that she had hit a person, drove on. when she reached the tollbooths at the triborough bridge, she told a police officer that she had struck something, and they directed her to return.  no charges were filed against either driver. the police said they did not know why the victim had been walking on such a busy highway at night.
