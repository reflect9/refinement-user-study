paid notice: deaths   hirsch, oliver   hirsch--oliver, 60, died january 15 in nyc. he was exhibitions director of the raices latin music museum, professor of exhibition design in the graduate program at nyu and president of hirsch and associates fine arts services, inc. from vietnam war resister to coal miner, musician, art professional and youth mentor, he spent his life fulfilling his intense curiosity and reinventing himself at every opportunity. he is survived by his wife marcia gomide, two daughters, jennifer and annie hirsch, two grandchildren, george oliver and chloe zenobia davis, and two brothers. the family asks that donations in mr. hirsch's name be made to the oliver v. hirsch youth and museum studies fund at harbor conservatory, 1 e. 104, #
