philharmonic casts 'my fair lady'   the cast of the new york philharmonic's semistaged performance of ''my fair lady'' from march 7 to 10 has been announced: kelli o'hara (''the pajama game'') will play eliza doolittle, and kelsey grammer (''frasier'') will play prof. henry higgins.  rounding out the cast will be charles kimbrough as col. pickering, brian dennehy as alfred p. doolittle and marni nixon as mrs. higgins.
