paid notice: deaths   morrow, sidney s.   morrow--sidney s., 89, died sunday, january 7, 2007 in belmont, california, formerly of tamarac, florida and the bronx. beloved husband of the late dorothy, loving father of sandy davidson (doug) and lennie cruze (ted) and beloved grandfather of emily and lydia cruze and matt and julia davidson. a world war ii veteran, who served as an officer with patton's third army in europe, sid was an avid amateur tennis player, actor and singer. active in progressive, political causes, schmulie was a spirited man who will be missed by all. in lieu of flowers, donations gratefully accepted to the susan g. komen breast cancer foundation or the southern poverty law center. grave side services on friday, january 12 at 11am at mount moriah cemetery, fairview, new jersey.
