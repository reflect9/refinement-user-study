paid notice: deaths   perloff, sondra   perloff--sondra, 78, born and raised in new york city, died thursday, january 18, at her home in longboat key, florida. she was married to milton perloff in 1949, and raised three daughters. after her children were grown, she and her husband moved to sarasota in 1977. sandy is survived by two daughters and sons-in-law, linda and bob freundlich of key largo, florida; sheila and david eddison of topsfield, massachusetts; son-in-law james fossett of delmar, new york; and grandchildren eric freundlich, melissa and sara eddison, and jeffrey fossett. she was predeceased by her husband and her daughter janet. services will be at riverside-nassau north chapels in great neck, ny, on tuesday, january 23, at 10am. burial will follow at the family plot in elmont, ny. the family will be receiving friends at her home at seaplace on longboat key on sunday, january 28, at 2pm. memorial contributions can be made to the university at albany foundation, memo the janet d. perloff fund, established in memory of her late daughter to fund scholarships for students in social work and public health. mail contributions to janet d. perloff fund school of social welfare ri 120, 135 western ave, albany ny 12222.
