paid notice: deaths   liberman, pearl   liberman--pearl. the board of trustees and staff of the brooklyn museum record with deep sorrow the passing of our trustee and great friend, pearl liberman, and extend our deepest sympathy to her family and friends. we are grateful for her devoted service as a trustee since 1974 and a member of the museum's community committee and asian art council. norman m. feinberg, chair arnold lehman, director
