michelle walter, cyrus hadidi   michelle lynn walter, the daughter of linda g. walter and robert l. walter of naples, fla., was married yesterday at the one & only ocean club on paradise island in the bahamas to cyrus stephan hadidi, the son of grazyna anna hadidi and nasser hadidi of colfax, wis. the rev. franklin knowles, a methodist minister, signed the marriage license, and the rev. brian scott, an evangelical lutheran minister and the brother-in-law of the bridegroom, led a religious ceremony.  the bride, 30, and the bridegroom, 33, work in los angeles, where she is an account director with euro rscg worldwide, the advertising agency, and he is a partner in jmb capital, a hedge fund. he is also on the board of a place called home, an after-school center for children there. she graduated from depauw university. he graduated from the university of california, berkeley, and received an m.b.a. from harvard.  the couple met in february 2004 on a southwest airlines flight from sacramento to los angeles that offered open seating. ''i remember seeing him walk down the aisle and thinking he was very handsome,'' ms. walter said. ''i thought, 'why doesn't someone like that ever sit next to me?' ''  when he took the seat right beside her, she remembered being ''very excited'' and then disappointed when he failed to utter a word.  ''halfway through the flight, after drinks were served, he started talking,'' ms. walter said. he had been holding back, mr. hadidi later revealed, as he attempted to gauge her interest. before going their separate ways they exchanged telephone numbers.  ''he called that week,'' ms. walter said, and they became a couple almost immediately.  weddings/celebrations
