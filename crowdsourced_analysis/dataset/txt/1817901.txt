minor league deal for difelice   the mets signed the journeyman catcher mike difelice to a minor league contract yesterday. difelice, 37, was added to the mets' major league roster in august when the backup catcher ramon castro went on the disabled list because of a sore left rib cage. in 15 games with the mets, difelice batted .080. castro returned for the playoffs, and difelice was left off the postseason roster.  michael s. schmidt  sports briefing: baseball
