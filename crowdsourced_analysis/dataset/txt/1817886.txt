national briefing | new england: massachusetts: commuter train kills 2 workers   two track workers were killed, and four were injured when a commuter train plowed into track maintenance equipment. the train, with 43 passengers, was headed to boston from lowell when it hit the equipment shortly before 2 p.m. ten passengers were taken to hospitals with minor injuries, and no crew members were hurt. scott farmelant, a spokesman for massachusetts bay commuter rail, said the crew was performing routine track maintenance work.  katie zezima
