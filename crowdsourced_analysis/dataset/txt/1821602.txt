metro briefing | new jersey: teaneck: bus hits and kills pedestrian   a man was struck and killed last night by a new jersey transit bus turning left from teaneck road onto state street, the teaneck police said. the man, whose identity was not immediately released, was declared dead at the scene around 8:30 p.m. the police said that the death appeared to be an accident and that they did not expect charges to be filed.  jennifer 8. lee
