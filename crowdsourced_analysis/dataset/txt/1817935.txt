paid notice: deaths   roosevelt, oliver w. jr   roosevelt--oliver w. jr, 79, died on january 9th after a long illness. son of oliver w. and verdery akin roosevelt, he was fine arts editor and music critic for the birmingham news. he is survived by his wife ann; son oliver w. iii (carol); daughters verdery, laura patterson (jim), nicky (judy jackson), and carolyn (bill gramby); grandsons nicholas w. and oliver w. iv; step-grandchildren reed (lisa) and megan patterson; and brother j. west.
