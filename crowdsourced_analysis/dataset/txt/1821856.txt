paid notice: deaths   fiorato, joelyn   fiorato--joelyn. peter martins and the new york city ballet mourn the passing of joelyn fiorato, beloved wife of our long-time music director and current conductor emeritus, hugo fiorato. an author and artist, joelyn had a larger-than-life spirit that made her a treasured member of the new york city ballet family. we extend our deepest condolences to hugo and the family. she will be missed.
