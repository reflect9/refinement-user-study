paid notice: deaths  castaldo, virginia   castaldo--virginia, at age 86, passed away peacefully at home, after a long illness, predeceased by her husband of 50 years, lee castle, band leader of the jimmy dorsey orchestra. also, recently predeceased by her beloved sister violeta velero hall, with whom she performed as the velero sisters in the 1940's in nyc, at the rainbow room, la conga and the latin quarter. virginia was a model and actress and was among many screen tested for the role of scarlett o'hara in ''gone with the wind''. she is survived and mourned by her nieces, loria parker and marguerite hall hansen, and grandnephew evan hansen. virginia leaves a legacy of great beauty as a person and as an artist of many paintings as well as originally written songs. her humor and laughter will ring forever in the hearts of the many who loved her. she will lie in repose tuesday, january 30th, 79pm, at romano funeral home, 725 e. gun hill road, bronx ny, 10467, phone: 718547-7799. funeral mass wednesday january 31st, 10:30 am at church of the immaculate conception, 754 e. gun hill road, bronx ny.
