national briefing | south: florida: new leader of juvenile justice department   gov. charlie crist named chief walter a. mcneil of the tallahassee police as secretary of the state department of juvenile justice. anthony schembri, a former new york city commissioner of corrections, held the post under former gov. jeb bush and was in charge a year ago when martin lee anderson, 14, died a day after being admitted to a juvenile boot camp in panama city. in november, seven guards and a nurse were charged with manslaughter in connection with martin's death. mr. mcneil, a democrat, said he would balance prevention and treatment programs with punishment.  christine jordan sexton
