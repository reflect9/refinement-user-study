paid notice: deaths   magnifico, anne shuttleworth. ''elegant annie''   magnifico--anne shuttleworth. ''elegant annie''. beloved wife and mother leaving husband george, daughter nicole, brother robert and many relatives and friends to miss her eternal gentleness and friendship. annie in her 68 years touched everyone she met with her infectious smile, her big blue eyes, and inner warmth. her magnetism was irresistible, drawing in as friends everyone she touched. we loved you while you were with us, we love you now, and we will never forget you. farewell...fly with the angels. born june 11, 1939 in amsterdam, new york. died january 17, 2008 in stuart, florida. a funeral for annie was held on saturday january 26, 2008 in northville, new york. a celebration of life party is planned in a few weeks in her honor in hewlett, ny. details will be available at northvillefuneralservice.com or by emailing george at mgnifico@optonline.net.
