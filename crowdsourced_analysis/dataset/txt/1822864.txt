metro briefing | new york: queens: hit-and-run leaves man dead   a 69-year-old man was killed in a hit-and-run accident yesterday while he was crossing a street in springfield gardens, the police said. the man, whose identity was not released last night, was walking across 140th avenue near 156th street about 6 p.m. when he was hit by a dark-colored jeep, which drove off. the man was declared dead on arrival at jamaica hospital medical center, the police said.  jennifer 8. lee
