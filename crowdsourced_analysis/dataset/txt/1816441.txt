paid notice: deaths   rukin, alice   rukin--alice, devoted mother of ann schwartz, barnett rukin and wendy gelman (steven gelman), adoring grandmother of laura schwartz (michael caruso), meryl schwartz (jeffrey salat), melissa schwartz (david muenzer), lauren gelman (kevin poulsen), allison gelman, michael rukin, laurence rukin and bryan rukin, and loving great-grandmother to benjamin, emily and sadelle, passed away peacefully in her sleep at the age of 96 on january 3, 2008. a strong, loving woman, she devoted herself entirely to her children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. her love of opera, piano, card games, conversation and laughter will always be remembered. her passionate belief in the importance of family will live on through her children, grandchildren and great-grandchildren. services will be held on sunday, january 5, 2008 at 9:30am, at riverside-nassau north chapels, 55 north station plaza, great neck, new york.
